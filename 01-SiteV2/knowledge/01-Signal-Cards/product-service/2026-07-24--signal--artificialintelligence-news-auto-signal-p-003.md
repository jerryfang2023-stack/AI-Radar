# Nvidia 用 Physical AI 框架解决医疗机器人数据难题：Medical Physics Simulation 发布

## 新闻事实

Nvidia 推出 Medical Physics Simulation 框架，将医疗机器人视为需要具身体验而非仅靠代码学习的 Physical AI 系统。

## 原文要点

- A physical AI system learns from what happens when a catheter meets a vessel wall, or when a robotic arm applies too much pressure to soft tissue.

## 价值描述

将医疗机器人视为需要具身体验而非仅靠代码学习的 Physical AI 系统。

## 可见原文片段

Physical AI is the term Nvidia and much of the robotics industry now use to describe machines that have to learn how the world behaves through contact, force, and consequence, rather than through text or images alone.
