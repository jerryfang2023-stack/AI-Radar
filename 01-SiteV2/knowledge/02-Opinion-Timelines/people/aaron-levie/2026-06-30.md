> 更多数据表明，AI的普及与就业之间的关系，与许多人的预期恰恰相反。Ramp发现，一家公司对AI的采用程度越高，其员工人数增长反而越多。在Box，我们最近对1600多家中型和大型企业进行了一项调查，结果也类似。58%的受访者预计未来三年员工人数会增加。有趣的是，在AI应用最成熟的群体中，这一比例攀升至79%。AI应用更深入的企业，预期未来员工人数的增长速度会高于其他企业。当然，那些最有能力采用AI的公司，往往也是业务正在增长的公司，这自然会带来员工人数的增加。所以，这个故事的重点并不在于采用AI就一定会带来增长。*但*最重要的启示是，相反的情况并未出现。几年前，人们担心那些最积极采用AI的公司会减少招聘。但实际上，这才是你真正应该预期发生的情况。如果一家公司因为将AI用于销售中的客户或市场情报分析而获得了更多客户，他们就会招聘更多而非更少的销售人员。如果你能比以前开发出多得多的软件，你最终会雇佣更多工程师，因为项目规模会变大，承接的项目也会更多。其他领域也是如此。

**原文摘录**

> 原始观点/摘要：More data is showing the opposite of what many people expected with AI adoption and jobs. Ramp found that the more AI adoption a company has the more their headcount grows. At Box, we recently did a survey of 1,600+ mid and large sized companies, and the findings were similar. 58% of respondents expected headcount to rise over the next three years. Interestingly, that figure climbs to 79% among the most mature adopters of AI. The more advanced AI adopters expected to grow their headcount at a greater rate in the future than others. Of course it's true that the companies that can afford to adopt AI the most are also the ones that likely are seeing growth in their business, leading to more headcount. So the point of the story isn't necessarily that by adopting AI you will inherently grow. *But* the most important takeaway is that the opposite is not proving out. The fears a couple years ago would have been that the companies adopting AI the most would be hiring fewer people. But in reality this is what actually you should expect to happen. If a company can get more customers because they use AI in sales for account or market intelligence, they hire more sales people not fewer. If you can build way more software than before, you end up hiring more engineers because the projects get bigger and you take on more.
