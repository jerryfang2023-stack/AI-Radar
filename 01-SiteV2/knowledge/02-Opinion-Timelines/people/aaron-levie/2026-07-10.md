**中文翻译**

> 精彩文章，探讨了当AI模型在企业间共享、并承载着行业大量智能时，未来竞争优势的演变动态。这将成为未来十年乃至更久，企业乃至整个经济面临的核心问题。如果AI在每一个行业——如法律、金融、医疗或生命科学——都基于最佳数据集进行训练，那么未来你该如何竞争，如何实现差异化？这是一个极佳的开放性问题，鉴于AI进展之快，我认为目前还无法完全预知答案。但归根结底，可以合理推断：如果智能变得充裕，且行业内任何人都能广泛获取，那么那些能最有效运用它，并依托一套随时间增值的数据和知识体系的企业，将占据强势地位。模型带来的智能、企业自有数据、这些数据与AI在工作流程中的结合，以及员工最终如何与这一系统互动以创造价值，这之间存在着一个巨大的强化循环。没有明显的迹象表明，这一模式会在某个垂直领域的所有企业中变得千篇一律，因为每家企业都会以不同的方式应对，正如它们目前在人才和工作流程上的做法一样。甚至可以说，那些做得最好的企业将获得复利效应，随时间推移加速扩大其优势。总的来说，这是一个极为有趣的问题，值得观察其未来如何演变。

**原文摘录**

> 原始观点/摘要：Great post on some of the dynamics to think through for the future competitive advantage in world when AI models are shared amongst firms and packing so much for the intelligence of that industry. This is going to become a core question for companies and the economy broadly over the next decade and beyond. If AI is trained on the best datasets in every single industry - like law, finance, healthcare, or life sciences - then how do you compete and differentiate in the future? This is a great open question that I don’t think is perfectly knowable right now because of how fast AI progress is happening. But ultimately it stands to reason that if intelligence is abundant and broadly available to anyone in a field, then the companies that effectively use it the best and against a set of data and knowledge that grows in value over time, will be in a strong position. There’s a huge reinforcing loop between the intelligence from models, a company’s own data, the connection of that data and AI in their workflows, and how employees ultimately interact with that system to create value. There’s no obvious point where this will become uniform across all companies in a vertical because each company will approach this in a different way, just as they already do with their talent and workflows. If anything, there will be compounding returns to those that do this best that accelerate their advantage over time.
