People often wonder why isn’t this like just deploying other forms of technology in the past, like cloud. Because something like cloud adoption affected a fairly concentrated set of users (developers and IT), and generally didn’t require a fundamental change to the workflows of employees to get the benefits of the new service being delivered on the cloud. At best you went to one training session and you were done. With agents, the work to implement them is not only highly technical, but they directly impact the underlying workflows that people participate in.
