**中文翻译**

> AI领域的竞争正逐渐演变为一场关于上下文的争夺战。AI的一切都旨在确保智能体尽可能高效运作，而这种高效性取决于智能体是否具备正确的领域专业知识、能否获取恰当的上下文及配套工具，并以用户易于交互、审查成果并将其融入后续流程的方式参与工作流。因此，那些能够在其智能体中捕获并利用最优质、最丰富上下文，且能为任务选择合适模型的平台，将成为智能体发挥最佳效能的阵地。只需观察编程智能体、法律智能体或客服智能体的大规模应用案例，便能窥见这种形态。这正是应用层AI的价值远不止于大语言模型包装壳的原因所在——其关键在于组织关键工作知识，并以受管控的方式维护这些知识，确保只有授权人员和智能体才能访问，同时持续优化智能体的上下文环境。随着时间推移，这一层级将能在多种模型间调度工作：利用前沿智能进行规划、协调与审查，并在大量中间任务中混合使用低成本模型（开源或闭源）。应用层还具备优势，可针对特定领域训练和开发自有专用模型。虽然永远不应押注于“苦涩的教训”的反面，但在许多情况下，采用接近前沿水平的基础模型并针对单一领域工作进行后训练，既能降低成本，也能提升特定任务的表现。最终，这一应用层也将成为工作流变革管理的主要发生地。

**原文摘录**

> 原始观点/摘要：The battle in AI is shaping up to be a battle for context. Everything in AI is about making sure that agents are effective as possible. That effectiveness comes down to whether the agent has the right domain expertise, access to the right context and tools to work with, and are involved in workflow in a way that users can easily interact with, review its work, and incorporate it into the rest of the process. As a consequence, the platforms that are able to capture and leverage the best and most context within their agents and be able to pick the right models for the task- will be the place where agents do their best work. You can just look at coding agents, legal agents, or support agents as examples of what this looks like at scale. This is why the applied AI layer has a lot more value than just being an LLM wrapper. The ability to organize the critical knowledge for the work being done, and maintain this knowledge in a governed way where only the right people and agents have access, and the ability to improve the context for agents more and more over time, is critical. Over time, this layer will be able to route work between a variety of models, leveraging frontier intelligence for planning and orchestration and review, and a mix of lower cost models (open or closed) for the large volume of work between these tasks. The applied layer is also in a good position to train and develop its own models as well that are purpose built for their domains. > 在企业中部署人工智能，远不止与聊天机器人互动那么简单，这无疑需要付出切实努力，使人工智能系统与它们所涉及的基础业务流程对齐，并推动实现预期成果。大多数工作流程并非为人工智能代理直接嵌入而设计。如今企业中的工作流程面临数据碎片化、代理无法连接的遗留软件系统、依赖机构知识而非文档化知识等问题。要大规模可靠地部署代理，你需要清理数据、现代化IT系统、制定评估方案、推动新终端状态流程的变革管理等等。这还包括设计人类在循环中的参与位置（这将意味着人们与工作流程交互的全新方式），以及明确公司新的知识产权形态。正因如此，众多应用型人工智能公司正在扩大实地工程部署工作，并推出部署公司，而实地工程部署角色将成为未来科技领域最关键的工作之一。在这方面，还有大量工作亟待完成。

**原文摘录**

> 原始观点/摘要：The deployment of AI in the enterprise beyond just interacting with a chatbot will unequivocally take real work to align AI systems to the underlying business processes they’re involved in and drive the desired outcomes. Most workflows weren’t designed for AI agents to just drop into. Workflows today in the enterprise deal with fragmented data, legacy software systems that agents can’t connect with, institutional instead of documented knowledge, and more. To deploy agents reliably at scale you need to get data cleaned up, modernize IT systems, figure out evals, drive change management for the new end state process, and so on. This also involves designing where humans remain in the loop (which will mean entirely new ways people interact with the workflows), and figuring out what a company’s new IP looks like. This is why so many applied AI companies are expanding FDE efforts and launching deploycos, and why the FDE role will be one of the most critical jobs in tech going forward.
