> 关于当前AI定价动态的好帖子。我们基本上会看到一种杠铃式的定价格局：一端是高成本的前沿模型，另一端是廉价但性能不错的开放或闭源权重模型。接下来的问题是如何最大化前沿模型的效率，同时提升廉价模型的质量和性能。这正是应用AI层发挥作用的地方。应用层可以根据当前工作负载随时路由到最佳模型，同时缓解高昂的令牌成本或较差的性能表现。你越贴近底层工作流程，就越能为特定业务流程优化模型的使用。这完全取决于对工作流程的理解、领域特定的功能设计元素、按客户或流程进行的评估、为智能体做好数据设置等等。总体而言，这对应用AI是有利的。

**原文摘录**

> 原始观点/摘要：Good post on the dynamics of AI pricing right now. We’re basically going to see a barbell dynamic on pricing between high cost frontier models and cheap but good open or closed weights models. Then the question is how do you maximize efficiency from the frontier models, and get better quality and performance of the cheaper models. That’s where the applied AI layer comes in. The applied layer can route to the best model at any time given the workload at hand, as well as mitigate either high token costs or worse performance. The closer you are to the underlying workflow, the better you can tune the use of models for a given business process. That’s all about understanding the workflows, domain specific FDEs, evals by customer or process, getting data setup well for agents, and more.
