**中文翻译**

> 我有机会进行了两次IPO （ Meta和Dropbox ）。神话般的财富往往会放大更深层次的欲望，而不是创造新的欲望。主流的叙述是： “早期员工赚很多钱，买豪宅去海滩”。对于一些人：是的，他们想放松一下。太好了，我为他们感到高兴。但对于很多人来说，这是一个做更疯狂、更古怪事情的机会。开始新事物。资助新事物。让硅谷的疯狂循环继续下去。 在接下来的几个月里，有很多人拥有流动性，这将是一件很棒的事情。继续！

**原文摘录**

> I have had the chance to go through two IPOs (Meta and Dropbox). Fabulous wealth tends to amplify deeper desires, not create new ones. The mainstream narrative is: "Early Employees make lots of money and buy mansions and go to the beach". For some: Yes, they want to chill. That is great. I am happy for them. But for a lot of folks, it is a chance to do even crazier and wackier stuff. Start new things. Fund new things. Keep the crazy loop of Silicon Valley going. It's going to be awesome to have a lot of folks with liquidity in the coming months. Onwards!.
