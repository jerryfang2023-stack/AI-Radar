> 原始观点/摘要：传统产品经理在适应AI原生构建时面临的最大挑战是缺乏魔法思维。十年来的框架、“敏捷”方法和指标痴迷导致了以约束为先、渐进式的思维。我过去在团队中通过让他们想象我们拥有100年后的技术，设想它能实现的体验，并以此倒推来对抗这种思维。这样产生的想法总是更具创意。有趣的是，未来的技术已经到来。去构建那个神奇的东西吧！曾任：Google产品负责人 - Gemini、Veo、Nano Banana。

**原文摘录**

> 原始观点/摘要：The biggest challenge for trad PMs trying to adapt to AI native building is a lack of magical thinking. A decade of frameworks, “agile” and metric obsession has led to constraint-first, incremental thinking. I used to combat this on my teams by asking them to imagine we had tech from 100 yrs in the future, the experiences it would enable and working backward from that. the ideas would always be far more creative. the funny thing is, that technology from the future is here. go build that magical thing!
