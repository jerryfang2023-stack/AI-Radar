**原文摘录**

> Right from the early days of Gemini, enterprises would get the quality/cost tradeoff wrong. They’d often start with the smallest, cheapest model. The rule of thumb we gave customers: Replacing a traditional ML model with an LLM - start small, cos you already know what good looks like. Building something new - start with the most capable model. Think magically. Figure out what’s actually possible first. Once they had a high-quality working application, we’d help them move to a smaller model while maintaining quality.
