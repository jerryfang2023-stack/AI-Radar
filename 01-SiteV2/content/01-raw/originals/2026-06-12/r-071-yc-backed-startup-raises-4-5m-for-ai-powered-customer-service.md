Flai is officially scaling up its mission to revolutionize car dealerships, announcing a successful $4.5 million seed funding round.
The company's core function is to bridge a critical gap for auto retailers: maximizing customer outreach and minimizing lost opportunities by utilizing custom-built, omni-channel AI technology. While many firms are active in this sector, Flai stands out by creating its voice systems entirely from the ground up, specifically adapting them to manage phone calls, digital messages, and emails within the retail environment.
This foundational investment was led by First Round Capital , with participation from key backers including Y Combinator , RedBlue Capital , Liquid 2 Ventures (Joe Montana's fund), and Innovation Endeavors . The new capital will be used to accelerate the expansion of their proprietary AI platform, which was initially refined through direct, on-the-ground engagement with hundreds of dealership locations.
Congratulations to co-founders Ari Polakof , Alen Polakof , and Juan Francisco Alzugaray , and the entire Flai team on reaching this significant milestone!
