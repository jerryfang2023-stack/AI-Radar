Sema4.ai Enterprise Edition revolutionizes how large organizations harness AI, offering a comprehensive platform to build, deploy, and manage enterprise-scale AI agents. Built on our SAFE (Secure, Accurate, Fast, Extensible) framework, it operates securely within your AWS Virtual Private Cloud (VPC), ensuring maximum control and compliance.
Transforming complex, high-value work across your organization, Sema4.ai Enterprise Edition empowers users, from technical experts to those without coding experience, to develop intelligent agents. This is achieved through natural language Runbooks and seamless integration with existing enterprise systems and data.
From intelligent document processing to data-driven decision making, AI agents built on our platform enhance productivity and operational efficiency while maintaining the highest standards of security and governance.
Key Features:
Secure: All operations within your AWS VPC, leveraging existing security protocols
Accurate: Multi-modal document understanding and zero-copy access to your enterprise data
Fast: Create agents in minutes using natural language Runbooks, no coding required
Extensible: Comprehensive SDK and pre-built actions for enterprise app integration
Highlights
Create powerful AI agents in minutes, no coding required: Sema4.ai's natural language Runbooks allow business users to build sophisticated AI agents without programming knowledge. Our Studio provides pre-built actions and leading language models, enabling rapid development of agents that process documents, analyze data, and make informed decisions, adapting quickly to business needs.
Comprehensive AI solution for enterprise-wide transformation: Integrate powerful Document Intelligence and Dynamic Data Access capabilities. Process complex, unstructured data from various sources, automate document-centric tasks, and leverage zero-copy access to your enterprise data. Enable data-driven decision-making and predictive analytics across your organization, all within a single, secure platform.
Uncompromising security and control for enterprise AI: Operate entirely within your AWS Virtual Private Cloud, ensuring maximum data protection and compliance. Leverage existing security protocols, integrate with SSO, and maintain full control over your AI agents' lifecycle. SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant, meeting stringent enterprise requirements.
