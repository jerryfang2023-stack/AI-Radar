# Vertical AI founders share best practices for design partnerships ...

## clean_text

Vertical AI founders are thinking differently about design partnerships.
Vertical AI founders are thinking differently about design partnerships.
At our Vertical Collective Roundtable last week in SF, 10 founders spanning healthcare, real estate, auto, veterinary, and more weighed in.
Several shared best practices emerged:
1. Faster Iteration: Leverage AI prototyping to show responsiveness + ability to do what they can't. Weekly pushes turn skeptics into engaged partners.
2. Target Services Spend: BPO budget, common in most industries + fertile ground for AI, is a great way to capture revenue while refining product scope.
3. Balance Warm vs. Cold: Balance warm intros (which reflect access) with cold outreach to validate genuine, extensible market pull vs. AI tire-kicking.
4. Ideal Partner Count: Consensus around 5-10 design partners for mid-market+, ensuring sufficient diversity & avoiding work dilution / product creep.
5. Evolution from MVP: Prototypes are dime-a-dozen with AI. Distill to solutions that can grow fast + tie to natural next steps in your target vertical workflow.
Our Roundtable emphasized that the best Vertical AI design partnerships:
+ Start earlier (MVPs are cheap + legacy industry AI appetite is real)
+ Iterate faster (allowing them to be deeper, less transactional)
+ Can generate revenue sooner (eating services spend)
+ But still risk being easier to get & harder to convert (AI tire-kicking)
