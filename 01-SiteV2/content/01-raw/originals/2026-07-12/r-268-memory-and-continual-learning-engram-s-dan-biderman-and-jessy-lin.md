# Memory and Continual Learning: Engram's Dan Biderman and Jessy Lin

## clean_text

Dan Biderman and Jessy Lin, co-founders of Engram, are building a neolab around memory and continual learning, which they call two sides of the same coin. Their contrarian premise: instead of stuffing ever-larger prompts into the context window or bolting on RAG, bake a team's knowledge directly into the model's weights, so it knows your company the way an employee of several years does. The payoff: matching or beating frontier models while consuming up to 100x fewer tokens. Working with partn
