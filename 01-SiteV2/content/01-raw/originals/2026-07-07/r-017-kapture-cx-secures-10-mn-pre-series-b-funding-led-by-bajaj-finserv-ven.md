Bengaluru | June 30, 2026 : Kapture CX , the verticalized full-stack agentic AI platform , has successfully closed its $10 million pre-Series B funding round .
Lead Investor: Bajaj Finserv Ventures (BFSV), part of Bajaj Finserv, making its first-ever investment in AI .
Other Participants: Existing investors Cactus Venture Partners and India Alternatives reaffirmed their conviction in Kapture’s growth story.
Use of Funds: Expansion into global markets, continued investment in R&D and product development , and targeting 5x revenue growth in the coming years.
Market Context
Large enterprises have invested heavily in AI but struggle to see real returns .
Market cluttered with point solutions from multiple providers, making adoption complex.
Enterprises need a full-stack agentic AI player that deeply understands industry workflows and delivers custom solutions .
Kapture CX fills this gap by offering a single agentic OS platform that integrates models, agentic layers, and user interfaces.
Platform Impact
Trusted by 1,000+ enterprises across 18 countries .
Clients include Bajaj Finance , multiple companies within the Tata Group and Reliance Group , as well as global consulting firms and hyperscalers .
Proven ability to help enterprises save millions of dollars through agentic workflows.
Lakshmi Iyer, Group President – Investments and MD & CEO, Bajaj Alts: “We are launching the AI Opportunities Fund to invest across the emerging AI ecosystem. In Kapture, we found a solution that demonstrably works at scale. Its deep-tech capabilities, model training expertise, and strong understanding of enterprise workflows position it strongly within the evolving AI landscape.”
Mangesh Anaokar, Fund Manager – AI Investments, Bajaj Alts: “Our conviction was strengthened by Kapture’s track record of delivering impact at scale across BFSI, retail, travel, energy, and consumer sectors. We are excited to work alongside Shesh, Vikas & team in shaping its next phase of growth.”
Sheshgiri Kamath, Co-founder & CEO, Kapture CX: “Since our Series A in 2023, Kapture has grown 4x and achieved profitability—rare in the AI economy. Having Bajaj Finserv Ventures lead this round is powerful validation of our value proposition. For enterprises beyond the experimentation phase, Kapture is where real value begins.”
Growth & Expansion
Revenue has grown 4x since Series A (2023) .
Achieved profitability , a rare milestone among AI-first companies.
Fresh capital will fuel global expansion and product innovation .
Targeting 5x revenue growth over the next few years.
About Kapture CX
Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Bengaluru , Kapture CX is a verticalized, full-stack agentic AI platform built to orchestrate complex enterprise workflows.
Through deep-tech capabilities, Kapture integrates:
AI agents .
Operational intelligence .
Human oversight .
This unified system enables enterprises to run high-stakes operations at scale , transforming workflows across industries.
