Machine and Computer vision solutions are enabling better analytics and decision making. There are tradeoffs between where you choose to put your CV compute. For example, moving computer vision tasks to the edge, where the data is created and/or captured, brings significant value to users: real-time processing by removing the dependency on a data center or cloud connection; enhanced privacy and security by minimizing data movement; increased scalability by bringing analytics into remote or bandwidth-constrained environments.
