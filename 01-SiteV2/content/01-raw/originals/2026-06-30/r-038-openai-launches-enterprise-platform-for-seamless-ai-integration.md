I recently read an article on OpenAI’s new Frontier platform, which prompted me to reflect on the direction of the ecosystem.
https://lnkd.in/g_Y3dd9G
Frontier represents a significant advancement. It addresses the identity problem and establishes a "Shared Business Context," enabling agents to understand what your business does.
However, knowing what to do is not the same as executing it efficiently.
Frontier equips agents with Knowledge (Data, Docs, Permissions), while the Process Intelligence ecosystem provides the Wisdom of Execution (Flow, Bottlenecks, Compliance).
If Frontier facilitates the onboarding of the agent, Process Intelligence oversees the Performance Review by addressing questions such as:
- Are they taking the long way around?
- Are they creating rework downstream?
- Are they compliant with the actual process?
Frontier manages the Agent, while Process Intelligence manages the Value. Tell me what you think.
