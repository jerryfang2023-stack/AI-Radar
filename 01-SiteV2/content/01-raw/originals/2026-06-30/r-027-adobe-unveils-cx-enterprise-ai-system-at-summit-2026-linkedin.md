Here’s a breakdown of the Day 2 from Adobe Summit 2026:
🏛️ Adobe CX Enterprise - The Flagship Launch
Adobe unveiled CX Enterprise, a new end-to-end agentic AI system designed to simplify how businesses manage their entire customer lifecycle - from acquiring and engaging prospects to driving conversion and lasting loyalty. It brings together AI agents, agent skills, and Model Context Protocol (MCP) endpoints under an intelligence and governance layer to deliver reliable, auditable agentic workflows.
🤝 Expanded Partner Ecosystem
Adobe announced a major expansion of its agentic ecosystem across leading technology companies, agencies, and system integrators.
A notable deep partnership: Adobe is teaming with NVIDIA to integrate the NVIDIA OpenShell secure runtime and NVIDIA Nemotron open models, combining security and governance with CX Enterprise’s marketing intelligence layer - enabling governed agents for regulated industries.
✍️ GenStudio Brand Intelligence (Content Supply Chain)
Adobe announced major updates across Adobe GenStudio to deliver an agentic content supply chain that connects enterprise context, brand intelligence, and AI agents across planning, creation, activation, delivery, reporting, and insights workflows. This is the next level of brand marketing, governance, scale, and marks a significant shift between AI, marketing, and brand.
🎯 CX Enterprise Coworker + Customer Engagement Innovations
Embedded within CX Enterprise is the CX Enterprise Coworker, positioned as an orchestrator rather than a point solution - capable of monitoring signals, recommending next-best actions, and executing experiences across channels in real time, with humans kept in the loop throughout.
New capabilities bundled in include:
Adobe Engagement Intelligence, a decisioning engine optimized for customer lifetime value; Adobe Journey Optimizer Loyalty for gamified loyalty experiences; Adobe CX Analytics, a unified intelligence layer extending to LLM-powered interfaces; and an expanded Real-Time CDP profile capability that harmonizes unstructured and structured data.
👁️ Brand Visibility - AI Discovery Surface: Adobe’s brand visibility solution addresses a problem most CX leaders are starting to feel acutely: AI-powered browsers and chatbots are rapidly becoming the first point of contact between brands and customers. Adobe’s own data showed that AI traffic to U.S. retail sites climbed 269% year-over-year in March 2026.
The solution addresses the dual challenge of ensuring brands are visible, accurate, and trusted across AI discovery surfaces, while deepening direct engagement with customers on owned properties.
The overarching theme:
Adobe’s goal is to move CX, marketing, and content creation from assistants that respond to prompts, to orchestrated agents that automate tasks, to full-fledged AI coworkers.
