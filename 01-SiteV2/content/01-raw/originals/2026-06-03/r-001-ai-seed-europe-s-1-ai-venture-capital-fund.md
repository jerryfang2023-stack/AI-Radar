INVESTMENT
AI Seed is an award-winning AI venture capital fund with Europe’s largest and best-performing portfolio of early-stage AI investments.
Since 2017, our team of successfully exited founders has built Europe’s largest and best-performing portfolio of pure-play AI companies and achieved the industry’s leading track record of AI investment returns.
SUPPORT
AI Seed is run by founders who have successfully built and sold their own startups. We understand the unique needs of AI companies, and we provide specialist support to help founders achieve their vision:
Access to some of the world’s best AI entrepreneurs
Help acquiring the best AI talent for your startup
Introductions to AI commercial partners and customers
Technical and operational support for AI scaling and growth
Business support preparing for future acquisition or IPO
We are active in both the European and Silicon Valley AI startup ecosystems and offer unique international support to European AI startups seeking to expand into the US.
TEAM
Michael Axelgaard (Founder and Managing Partner)
Michael has led AI Seed's investment decisions since its launch in 2017. Previously, he built his own medical software startup Inmedico and sold it to Natus Medical.
He holds a BSc in Engineering from UC Berkeley and an MPhil in Scientific Computing and Chemical Engineering from Cambridge University and is active in both the European and Silicon Valley AI startup ecosystems.
Jacques de Cock (Operations and Managing Partner)
Jacques has over 30 years of startup experience. Previously, he grew his own fintech software startup Patsystems from a six-man team to an IPO on the London Stock Exchange.
He holds a BA and an MA in Physics from Oxford University and an MBA from London Business School where he is Entrepreneur in Residence and is active in both the European and Silicon Valley AI startup ecosystems.
Steve Weis (Technical Analysis and Managing Partner)
Steve is a senior software engineer at AI cloud data platform Databricks. Previously, he built his own security software startup PrivateCore and sold it to Meta.
Steve holds a BS in Applied Mathematics from UC Berkeley, a BA in Computer Science from UC Berkeley and a PhD in Computer Science from MIT and is active in the Silicon Valley AI startup ecosystem.
General Partners and Support Staff
AI Seed’s Managing Partners are supported by General Partners who provide strategic advice and Principals who provide administrative support.
