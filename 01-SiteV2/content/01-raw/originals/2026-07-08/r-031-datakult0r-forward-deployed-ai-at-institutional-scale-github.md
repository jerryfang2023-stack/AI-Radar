Forward-Deployed AI at Institutional Scale
Overview
This repository documents the work and methodology of Philippe Küng , a Forward-Deployed AI Architect dedicated to upgrading the world's most critical institutions — sovereign governments, energy grids, and healthcare systems.
The core mission is to translate complex operational challenges into production-grade AI , owning the outcome from initial specification to global deployment.
🧬 The Forward-Deployed DNA
The approach combines five key pillars:
Technical Foundation: Production engineering honed at Crayon AG and MIT.
Emerging Tech: Expertise in LLMs, agentic systems, and state-of-the-art innovation.
Team Integration: Seamless on-site embedding with client teams.
Domain Fluency: Deep vertical expertise in Energy, Finance, and Healthcare.
Startup Spirit: Lean product development and rapid ROI focus.
🛠 Framework for On-Site Execution
On-Site Immersion: Mapping "unwritten rules" and workflow friction points.
Technical Specification: Translating qualitative bottlenecks into engineering KPIs.
Rapid Prototyping: Iterative loops: Talk to users → Write code → Test → Repeat.
📈 Evidence of Impact
1. Modernizing the National Grid (NTE)
Architected a cloud-native data warehouse on Azure Synapse integrating real-time industrial sensor data for grid monitoring.
Outcome: Enabled fact-based governance for critical national infrastructure.
2. Sovereign-Level Compliance (DNV)
Developed DATE (Digital Access to Experts), an NLP engine for vessel certification inquiries.
Outcome: 82% automation of inquiries, saving approximately $200k annually .
3. Healthcare Quality & Safety Audits
Built a Semantic Search solution on AKS to match auditor observations to accreditation standards.
Outcome: Significantly reduced surveyor errors and audit time.
🚀 Engineering the Technology Frontier
Project Guardian: Advanced RAG
Linking incident reports to 16,000 HSE controls for the Downer Group.
Impact: 80% accuracy in identifying safety controls in seconds.
Project Chimera: Multi-Agent Automation
Developed a multi-agent framework for financial compliance (FinSA, AML, KYC).
Impact: 60% reduction in manual portfolio review time.
Project Citadel: Production-Grade LLM Ops
Deployed secure, air-gapped generative AI for a National Ministry of Economic Development.
Impact: Ensured sovereign data control with high-performance 70B parameter models .
