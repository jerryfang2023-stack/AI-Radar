Peraton Unveils Peraton[x] ™ : The First True Enterprise Agentic AI Platform Built for the Nation’s Most Critical Operations and Missions
Share
Peraton[x] TM transforms enterprise operations to unleash their full power
RESTON, Va.--( BUSINESS WIRE )-- Peraton has released Peraton[x] TM , a breakthrough enterprise agentic AI platform that redefines what artificial intelligence can do for organizations operating at the highest stakes . Peraton[x] TM will evolve the future of the human enterprise and deliver material implications to the efficiency and effectiveness of every government agency and operation. The technology does not replace people, it supercharges them.
Until Peraton[x] TM , no true enterprise agentic AI existed. Existing solutions treat AI as a tool embedded within systems rather than as a core operational capability. They require specially trained operators, take weeks or months to deploy, and are constrained in mission scope. Peraton[x] TM changes all of that.
“Our AI development team has delivered something truly extraordinary. Peraton[x] TM is a first-of-its-kind agentic AI platform that sets a new standard in performance excellence,” said Peraton Chairman and CEO Steve Schorer. “With its ability to instantaneously deploy, process, and make sense of large sets of disparate data, Peraton[x] TM will fundamentally change how customer agencies scale, accelerate decision-making, and drive program execution. By bringing this technology to market, we're putting unprecedented speed and capability directly in the hands of those who serve citizens every day.”
Developed inside Peraton Labs — the company’s research and development (R&D) powerhouse whose people, facilities, and expertise trace their lineage to Bell Labs — Peraton[x] TM is the first agentic AI platform capable of optimizing how any enterprise runs its operations in real time, deployable in as little as hours, and programmable by anyone in plain English. Peraton[x] TM is what happens when you fuse an R&D lab that has been tackling DARPA-hard challenges for years with teams that have decades of firsthand mission experience on the U.S. government’s most sensitive operations.
“Peraton[x] TM is revolutionizing the human-enterprise relationship and how organizations optimize themselves in real time,” said Todd Borkey, Peraton’s Chief Technology Officer. “It puts the power of the organization into the hands of the users of Peraton[x] TM , giving them superhuman capabilities that they never had before.”
Peraton[x] TM includes a variety of differentiating features that provide organizations unique capabilities. Find out more at peraton.com/x .
Program management. Supercharged
Its AI-Powered PMO Suite multiplies the power of a project management office one hundredfold. It is a full-spectrum, composable AI platform that ingests, analyzes, and models the full range of unstructured data via plain-language prompts, delivering predictive intelligence, continuous risk assessment, spatial and temporal analysis, and enterprise digital twin capabilities. It shifts organizations from reactive data management to true AI-orchestrated operations.
Infinite applications for every team.
Fast, adaptive, and accessible to even non-technical users, Peraton[x] TM operates across an unprecedented array of mission sets, even beyond program management. These include intelligence and situational awareness, program and portfolio oversight, acquisition and compliance, risk management and prediction, operational automation, document forensics and discovery, financial management and forecasting, and spatial and temporal analysis through enterprise digital twin modeling.
High-stakes reliability for missions that matter.
Reflecting Peraton’s history of tackling some of America’s most consequential national security missions, Peraton[x] TM is engineered to handle even the highest-stakes data environments—explainability-focused, hallucination-mitigating, and security-minded by design. Every analytical output is fully traceable to its source material: no black-box conclusions, no unverifiable assertions.
Built for secure and sophisticated environments.
Peraton[x] TM is built on Zero Trust architecture from the ground up, with multi-factor authentication, end-to-end encryption, and role-based access control. The platform is FedRAMP Moderate compliant with a clear path to FedRAMP High. Peraton[x] TM is vendor-agnostic, data-agnostic, and contract-agnostic — adapting to existing enterprise IT environments from day one, with no re-engineering required. It puts customers’ missions first, featuring a flexible rather than rigid data ontology and enabling customers to retain their outputs regardless of future Peraton[x] TM use.
About Peraton
Peraton is a next-generation technology and national security company that drives missions of consequence. As one of the world’s leading mission capability integrators and transformative enterprise IT providers, we serve as a valued partner to the government, delivering trusted, highly differentiated solutions to protect our nation and allies from threats across digital and physical domains. Learn how we help sustain your way of life at peraton.com.
