SOCAR Türkiye saves 7,500+ hours annually with Copilot-based chatbot and AI agents</h1> "> SOCAR Türkiye saves 7,500+ hours annually with Copilot-based chatbot and AI agents

Energy company SOCAR Türkiye wanted to reduce the time lost to repetitive, manual tasks like retrieving information, approving travel, and responding to customer queries.
Working with Microsoft Copilot Studio and Microsoft Foundry, SOCAR Türkiye built its own chatbot, s.e.d.a.+, to assist with these tasks, as well as building its own custom AI agents.
As a result, the company is benefitting from faster response times, reduced manual expenditure, and savings of 7,500-plus hours each year.
SOCAR Türkiye is a proudly people-first energy company, prioritizing job security, career opportunities, and company culture. To further enhance employee experience and operational efficiency, the company identified opportunities to simplify processes and enable its people to focus more on value adding activities.
As part of that approach, SOCAR Türkiye identified certain specific areas that needed addressing.
For example, the Finance team was struggling to obtain payment details without expending resource on manual queries and checks. The Legal team had no self-service solutions, meaning that regulatory queries were time-consuming and slowed down compliance. Corporate Services was relying on an outdated ticketing process for approving facility, travel, and administrative requests. Finally, HR was losing huge amounts of resource to onboarding and training support, answering frequent FAQs, and gathering internal feedback.
SOCAR Türkiye needed a way to automate some of these tasks, streamlining workflows and freeing up team members to focus on providing the best possible service to customers.
“Once someone in our business starts to benefit from Copilot, word spreads to everyone. They say, ‘This is making my work easier,’ and new demand keeps coming.”
Mustafa Bora Özdemir, Digital Transformation Group Director, SOCAR Türkiye
Bringing energy to the workforce
Using Microsoft Copilot Studio and Microsoft Foundry, SOCAR Türkiye designed and built its own chatbot, s.e.d.a.+. What makes s.e.d.a.+ unique is its deeply integrated with Türkiye’s SAP, internal HR systems, Microsoft Teams, IT service management platforms, and corporate workflow tools. This allows employees not only to retrieve information but also to trigger end-to-end processes, such as initiating requests, approving transactions, and executing workflows directly within the chatbot.
After building s.e.d.a.+, SOCAR Türkiye went on to develop discipline-specific agents to assist with Finance, Legal, Corporate Services, HR, Procurement, and IT. These customized agents brought dedicated support to daily tasks that team members most frequently faced.
Built for every team’s needs
s.e.d.a.+ was rolled out as a variety of discipline-specific chatbots, each allowing employees to use natural language, in multiple languages, to get instant answers and automate tasks.
As Mustafa Bora Özdemir, Digital Transformation Group Director at SOCAR Türkiye, notes, “Once someone in our business starts to benefit from Copilot, word spreads to everyone. They say, ‘This is making my work easier,’ and new demand keeps coming.”
For Finance: s.e.d.a.+ can retrieve an IBAN, check payment status, or initiate finance-related requests. It also facilitates CapEx approval flows, allowing managers to approve requests and trigger SAP PO creation directly from Teams.
For Legal: s.e.d.a.+ can provide scenario-based guidance on regulatory procedures and help plan compliance workflows, quickly giving employees answers and document templates.
For Corporate Services: s.e.d.a.+ handles facility maintenance or travel bookings by creating and routing support tickets automatically to the right teams.
For HR: s.e.d.a.+ can answer questions about policies, onboard new hires, and collect employee feedback using the appropriate HR systems. It’s also being used to support continuous development, mentoring, and internal feedback collection.
For Procurement: s.e.d.a.+ provides real-time material pricing and delivery estimates from SAP data. Managers can also approve purchase requests within the chat, reducing manual lookups and streamlining approval processes. The Procurement Bot module uses historical SAP data to deliver real-time material pricing and delivery estimates, improving decision making.
For IT:  s.e.d.a.+ is able to handle over 1,300 IT and support requests annually. As well as guiding users through troubleshooting steps, it’s also automating responses and triggering backend actions for password resets, access requests, and software installations.
Overall, s.e.d.a.+ covers 40-plus automated scenarios and is accessible via Teams, web portal, and mobile, ensuring a seamless user experience across platforms.
Built in partnership
To ensure successful deployment and adoption, SOCAR Türkiye worked closely in partnership with Microsoft to develop s.e.d.a.+ around its needs and people. For example, an on-site Microsoft Technology Center (MTC) workshop was initially conducted with participation from leaders across SOCAR Türkiye. This was followed by demo and brainstorming sessions, which helped SOCAR Türkiye identify key pain points and map them to Copilot-based solutions.
In addition, the company participated in an 8-week ‘AI Marathon’ program with Microsoft Türkiye and its Microsoft partner, Mindworks. The program featured around 30 Digital Champions from across all of SOCAR Türkiye’s business units, which involved them developing their own AI agents.
The company chose the core AI technology, Microsoft 365 Copilot and Azure OpenAI services, to ensure s.e.d.a.+ was built specifically for its specific business needs. The chatbot is delivering efficiencies across teams and now has nearly 2,300 active users.
Digital champions help drive success
SOCAR Türkiye’s AI deployment was driven by a dedicated Centre of Excellence (COE) that embedded digital champions across different business functions.
Rather than simply implementing AI and moving on, these champions are actively helping with adoption and ongoing optimization. “Our AI environment is expanding, and we now have many digital champions throughout the organization that are helping drive adoption,” says Özdemir.
In addition to identifying valuable AI use cases, SOCAR Türkiye’s digital champions have initiated change management initiatives so that everyone in the business feels comfortable with how AI is being deployed.
This is being combined with implementing programs like the GEN-D program. Designed as a digital capability development initiative, GEN-D has reached over 50 employees to date across SOCAR Türkiye, enhancing digital literacy and supporting enterprise-wide AI adoption. New cohorts are planned to further expand its impact across the organization.
“Our AI environment is expanding, and we now have many digital champions throughout the organization that are helping drive adoption.”
Mustafa Bora Özdemir, Digital Transformation Group Director, SOCAR Türkiye
The success of s.e.d.a+ has been heavily driven by how engaging and intuitive it is to use. For instance, non-technical teams can use it to access real-time data and trigger backend actions using natural language. It also proactively engages users with context-aware prompts, supporting decision making and further encouraging user engagement.
SOCAR Türkiye has also launched a department-based prompt library that curates effective prompts by function and scenario. This helps ensure teams standardize best practices and further encourages ongoing usage. In fact, monthly interactions with s.e.d.a+ are consistently growing and exceeding 400-plus sessions per month, while user satisfaction scores have reached 4.8 out of 5.0.
Over 6,000 hours saved each year
50% faster response times
For a business-wide impact
Since deploying AI in key parts of the business, SOCAR Türkiye has experienced numerous benefits, including:
30% reduction in time spent on manual tasks for finance teams, based on automating repetitive queries such as invoice tracking and IBAN lookups
Up to 50% faster response times in internal helpdesk and HR workflows, thanks to proactive messaging and real-time data retrieval
1,820 hours saved per year in legal, thanks to support with legal research, document preparation, and standardizing outputs for review
225 hours per year saved in procurement via AI-assisted form validation and procedure document guidance
Overall, SOCAR Türkiye’s discipline-specific agents have helped the business save 6,318 hours per year , which can now be redirected to higher-value work.
As a people-first energy company, SOCAR Türkiye needed a way to support its people with the tasks they face each day. s.e.d.a.+ delivers that and more—and with a user satisfaction score of 4.8 out of 5.0, employees are not just using the tool, but genuinely valuing it.
This success story demonstrates how AI can be scaled across departments and unify fragmented systems. But more than that, it shows how AI can act as a true strategic enabler of digital transformation.
