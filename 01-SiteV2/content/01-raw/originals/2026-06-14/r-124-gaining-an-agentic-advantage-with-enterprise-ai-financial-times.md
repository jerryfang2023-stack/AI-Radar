How can leaders leverage agentic AI responsibly?
With AI adoption now well established, the focus is on how companies can ensure they are getting the most from it. The next wave of AI innovation is being led by agentic AI, where autonomous agents are able to make increasingly complex decisions with minimal human interaction.
Ireland is a European hub for digital innovation and enterprise, as a result of its pro-business environment, skilled talent and access to EU markets. Dublin is home to major tech companies and leaders across sectors including financial services, life sciences, manufacturing and logistics. Early movers are already taking advantage of AI’s full potential to improve operational efficiency and deliver more personalised customer experiences.
How can business leaders stay ahead of the curve through agentic AI, while complying with evolving regulation and sustainability goals?
Bringing together decision makers in major industries including manufacturing, finance, energy, healthcare and retail, this evening event provided insights into successful agentic AI implementation. Discussions shared proven strategies and discussed how they are addressing challenges in Dublin and the broader Ireland and EU market, with sustainability considerations in mind.
