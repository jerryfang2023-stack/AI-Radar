# Build, scale, and monetize apps and agents with Microsoft Marketplace | All things Azure

## clean_text

Cyril Belikoff
At Microsoft Build, we are sharing how Microsoft Marketplace brings development, distribution, and monetization together— so apps and agents move beyond prototyping into real-world usage at scale. Microsoft Marketplace connects tens of thousands of cloud and AI solutions built by software development companies to millions of commercial customers.
For developers, Marketplace turns code into results.
Build using the models, tools, and services you need from Microsoft Marketplace—available through consistent, Azure-native workflows.
Scale with distribution of your app or agent—through Marketplace and in-product experiences—like Microsoft 365 Copilot, Microsoft Teams, and Microsoft Foundry.
Monetize with built-in global commerce and go-to-market channels that help drive demand and accelerate adoption.
As you build, scale, and monetize your apps and agents, Marketplace connects them to customer demand. Today, Microsoft Marketplace is launching intelligent discovery (in preview). Instead of relying on keyword search alone, customers can describe their use case in natural language, and Marketplace surfaces relevant solutions with side-by-side comparisons. That means better matching between what you build and how customers discover, evaluate, and buy.
New capabilities include smart search that automatically applies filters and refinements through an action-first experience spanning discovery and purchase. Chat-enabled evaluation and comparison help potential buyers ask questions, compare options, and share findings with their team—making it easier for your solution to move from consideration to adoption. Built on Microsoft Foundry with AI guardrails , the experience is designed to support safe, secure interactions.
Build: go from prototype to production
Microsoft Marketplace brings the core components of your AI stack—models, developer tools, and applications—directly into your Azure environment. With Marketplace, developers can discover, deploy, and manage these components through the same Azure-native workflows they already use, including GitHub-based source control, CI/CD, and automation.
For example, when evaluating models, you can source from leading providers like Anthropic, Cohere, or Mistral—available through Marketplace and Microsoft Foundry—alongside thousands of other open-source options. By sourcing your AI stack through Marketplace, provision components through a consistent workflow, accessed through a common SDK. You can build, evaluate, swap, or extend your stack—whether that’s testing a different model, adding a new tool, or integrating a different service—without reworking your architecture.
With Marketplace, everything is sourced, deployed, and managed in one place. Licensing, billing, and validation are handled end-to-end, so developer teams spend less time on integration overhead and more time building to ship faster.
Scale: publish once, get surfaced everywhere
Publishing through Marketplace does more than create a listing on the Marketplace storefront. It’s a distribution path across Microsoft products customers already use—in their flow of work. Your experiences get contextually surfaced to 95% of the Fortune 500 using Azure, 400 million monthly active Teams users, and 20 million paid Microsoft 365 Copilot seats.
Across the Microsoft stack, Marketplace supports how you want to deliver and monetize your app or agent. Across SaaS, containers, or managed applications—packaging, deployment, billing, and go-to-market are aligned from the start. This makes your app or agent easier to commercialize, easier for customers to adopt, and enterprise-ready at time of publication.
Build agents that integrate with Microsoft 365 Copilot to reach a rapidly growing user base. Microsoft validates compatibility, so customers can find, try, and buy through Marketplace with fast provisioning in the Microsoft 365 admin center.
“Because of Microsoft Marketplace, we’re able to deliver value to customers faster. Using a managed application in Marketplace, we can deploy directly into a customer’s Azure tenant with the right security controls already in place. This gives our customers full data control while simplifying onboarding through a one-click installation. Microsoft Marketplace is a key driver to our growth with a 6x increase in revenue.”
Guy Tonye
Head of Engineering, Zammo.ai
Monetize: turn distribution into growth
Microsoft Marketplace offers a commercial platform that connects discovery, purchase, and deployment—with multiple paths to monetize your app or agent. Sell direct, and let the storefront do the work, or scale through partners.
For product-led growth, Marketplace helps customers discover and deploy cloud and AI solutions in one flow. AI enhancements like intelligent discovery (in preview), match your app or agent to customers based on their needs. Marketplace accelerates your time to market by handling billing, payouts, and the end-to-end commerce flow—so you can focus on building while Microsoft manages transactions, procurement, and deployment
For partner-led growth, Marketplace gives you more routes to market through an ecosystem of more than 500,000 Microsoft partners. As customers increasingly rely on technology providers to lead their AI transformation, Marketplace is building more partner-led sales opportunities. This includes the recent expansion of multiparty private offers—now available across 30 countries in Europe, with Australia, Japan, and South Africa coming July 15, 2026. ( See blog .)
From build to customers
Microsoft Marketplace supports the full developer lifecycle—from sourcing your AI stack, to monetizing at enterprise scale. As you build the next generation of apps and agents, Marketplace helps turn your code into results.
Everything you need to get started is in the Marketplace GitHub repo .
We can’t wait to see what you build.
💻 Build → 🌍 Scale → 📈 Monetize = Microsoft Marketplace
