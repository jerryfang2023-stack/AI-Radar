# How Salesforce Partners Transform Businesses Into Agentic ...

## clean_text

By Phil Samenuk, Senior Vice President, Global Alliances & Channels and Outsourcing Service Providers, Salesforce
Agentic AI is more than just the next wave of innovation. It’s a revolutionary leap reshaping every industry. Every company fears it will remain a legacy enterprise while the rest of the world, and its competitors, become Agentic Enterprises where humans and agents drive customer success together. This isn’t just about innovation; it’s about unlocking limitless capacity and growth.
Becoming an Agentic Enterprise makes businesses faster and smarter by augmenting every employee with conversational AI agents and unified data to meet customer needs more efficiently. Salesforce, with its Agentforce 360 Platform, is guiding businesses across industries to the Agentic Enterprise future, where humans and AI agents work side by side—collaborating across every workflow, decision and customer interaction.
This is an enterprise-scale transformation that demands a new mindset. That’s why Salesforce’s trusted partner ecosystem is a core part of the solution—extending Salesforce technology and scaling services to help companies in every industry make this vision a reality. With over 1 million active certifications, Salesforce partners provide technical and industry expertise to help companies of all sizes evolve into Agentic Enterprises.
True transformation isn’t just about the technology; it’s about the partnership that guides the process. The following four stories are your guide. They highlight how Salesforce partners like Accenture, Deloitte Digital, NeuraFlash and PwC are helping companies like UKG, T.Rowe Price, Vivint and PepsiCo become Agentic Enterprises and make the shift from siloed systems to seamless human-AI collaboration.
How UKG Accelerates AI Strategy—With Help From Accenture
UKG, a leading global provider of HR, payroll and workforce management solutions, teamed up with Accenture and Salesforce to build an AI strategy specifically focused on enhancing customer service with goals to reduce time to issue resolution, improve accuracy and arm frontline managers with data to make quicker, better-informed decisions. As an AI-first company, UKG selected Agentforce as part of its customer experience transformation.
According to Bob DelPonte, UKG’s executive vice president and chief customer experience officer, with Accenture’s help, the company has scaled a suite of AI-powered capabilities from case-intake automation to proactive support agents embedded in the UKG Community, where customers connect and share knowledge. “The result is a more responsive, streamlined experience,” DelPonte says.
By combining advanced AI with powerful insights from the UKG platform and embedding AI into every layer of the customer journey—intelligent search, personalized recommendations, proactive support and real-time agent assistance—UKG is helping organizations that use its HR, payroll and workforce management solutions with greater intelligence, efficiency and empathy. Building on its track record, UKG is accelerating its momentum, pioneering new innovations and delivering an even more differentiated, people-centered customer experience.
“At this point, my team has guided over 20 Agentforce deployments that aren’t just pilots or under development but actually out there in use,” says Louise Barrere, senior managing director, center for advanced AI at Accenture.
Deloitte Digital Implements High-Impact Agentforce Use Cases for T. Rowe Price
T. Rowe Price, a global asset management firm and leader in retirement, saw AI agents as the logical next step to boost productivity and put its data to better use. But taking full advantage of the new digital workforce required outside expertise. Like many asset managers, the firm aimed to achieve enhanced client personalization, faster response times and improved operational efficiency to surface better insights and drive action. The firm turned to Deloitte Digital, a long-time Salesforce collaborator with a deep understanding of the financial services industry and Agentforce.
“Teaming with Deloitte Digital has been instrumental in helping T. Rowe Price successfully implement Agentforce,” says Jacob Mathews, T. Rowe Price’s head of global distribution technology. “Together we recognized the transformative potential of agentic AI, and this shared vision laid the groundwork for a highly aligned collaboration.”
T. Rowe Price and Deloitte Digital mapped workflows, identified friction points and designed agentic solutions tailored to the firm’s client engagement model. Rather than starting with client-facing scenarios, T. Rowe Price worked to employ AI agents to take on time-consuming tasks so sales teams could spend more time with clients.
“At Deloitte Digital, our approach is to start with high-value use cases, but our real strength lies in designing AI solutions that evolve with our clients’ needs,” Nitin Dua, a principal at Deloitte Consulting LLP, says. “As one of the largest Agentforce implementors, Deloitte Digital brings both technical depth and industry insight to help companies like T. Rowe Price realize the full potential of agentic AI.”
The AI agents are designed to surface information and make suggestions while leaving client communication to humans. When first working with AI agents, Dua recommends integrating these agents as co-sellers within the sales process. “It’s important to get started, even with a few targeted use cases, and then iteratively expand as the agents learn and demonstrate accuracy in their responsibilities,” Dua explains.
NeuraFlash Scales AI Innovation at Vivint
Vivint, a smart home and security leader and long-time Salesforce customer, wanted to use agentic AI to give customers personalized 24/7 support. The company needed an advanced AI solution to handle complex technical questions from users and manage troubleshooting that involved multiple technologies. The company turned to NeuraFlash, part of Accenture, as a trusted advisor to help pilot a custom agent that works seamlessly within the Salesforce platform to give Vivint’s human agents deeper insights and boost customer service.
“We created a virtual agent that could be as effective as our best customer care trouble-shooting agent,” Tony Gilvear, Vivint’s vice president of technology, says. Personalized device troubleshooting and payment inquiries are now live in Vivint’s mobile app, featuring seamless handoffs to humans when needed.
“Vivint was among the first to adopt Agentforce, going live with troubleshooting and payments. With NeuraFlash, the company built seamless handoffs to humans with full context—setting the pace for how agentic technology transforms customer service,” says Tom Hebner, vice president of Salesforce Agentforce practice at NeuraFlash.
Vivint, like other companies at the forefront of agentic AI, learned the importance of the right Agentforce partner to guide a successful implementation. “We love NeuraFlash because it is a company of thought leaders and delivers quality solutions fast,” Gilvear says. “The team listens, understands our business and partners with our amazing talent to build better than either could do independently.”
PwC Guides PepsiCo’s Journey to Enterprise-Wide AI Transformation
PepsiCo has only recently begun working with Agentforce. But as one of the world’s largest food and beverage brands, PepsiCo has ambitions to match its size. The company envisions using AI agents to improve customer interactions by resolving common issues, checking delivery statuses or recommending products that boost store traffic.
To achieve these goals, PepsiCo worked with PwC, which spent five years implementing Salesforce-enabled retail execution and CRM solutions. “They’re bringing expertise on how we can take our Salesforce implementation to the next level with Agentforce,” says Shyam Venkat, PepsiCo’s chief digital and information office for North America.
A key priority is modernizing its call centers and unifying the technology stack that drives the enhanced Customer Contact and Experience Center. PepsiCo is also eyeing agents to tailor promotions store by store, recommend product mixes and eventually orchestrate supply chains to service its vast equipment fleet.
“Having a bold strategy and choosing the right software are essential,” said Ian Kahn, principal commercial and service excellence platform leader at PwC.
Partnerships Accelerate the Path to Becoming an Agentic Enterprise
No matter where customers are on their AI journey, whether building strategy, scaling enterprise transformations or developing targeted use cases, Salesforce partners provide expertise at every step. They help turn bold ambitions into measurable results, driving smarter decisions, better customer experiences and a faster path to becoming a true Agentic Enterprise.
Learn more about the Salesforce partner ecosystem and find the right partner to support your journey to becoming an Agentic Enterprise.
