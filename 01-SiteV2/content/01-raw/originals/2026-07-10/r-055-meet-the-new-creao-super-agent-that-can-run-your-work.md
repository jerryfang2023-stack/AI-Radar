Meet the New CREAO Super Agent That Can Run Your Work
CREAO just released a more powerful Super Agent. You chat. It delivers. And you can turn that great output into Agents that run on their own. Forever.
Imagine one AI tool where you can talk through any challenge and get real output delivered in real time. Where you can execute any task. And where you can turn that workflow into an Agent that runs, reliably, on time, every time.
And then you create the next one. Your capabilities compound, your work runs, your results get better over time because CREAO remembers your context, your preferences, your decisions.
That's CREAO's new Super Agent. You chat with it in plain language or by voice. It executes immediately, and when a conversation delivers, one click turns it into a reusable Agent with structured inputs, version control, integrations, and scheduled runs.
Chat. Create. Run it all.
Your Agents Work
Around the clock, on schedule, at scale.
A morning briefing compiled from your email and calendar — delivered before you open your laptop. A weekly analytics report formatted and ready to share. A client deliverable that used to take half a day, running monthly on its own. A daily competitive scan across ten sources, summarized and waiting for you. Proposals are generated, quotes are delivered, content is created, campaigns are adjusted, emails are answered, tickets are closed.
This is how digital work scales.
The AI-First Shift
CREAO delivers answers. CREAO runs tasks. But more than these, CREAO creates Agents. Output becomes an operation. An operation becomes a system. That's AI-First.
Welcome to the AI-First shift — where one person can do what used to require ten. CREAO is how you get there, moving from asking AI for help to running a workforce of Agents that operate your work. The distance is shorter than you think.
Who This Is For
The doers. The innovators. The relentlessly curious. The people who want to create, to grow, to lead. For those who think there is a better way.
A solopreneur scaling a business where one person has the output of a team. A professional ready to spend more time having the impact they know they can. A leader looking to dive into new spaces. A team lead who wants to empower their people to do more. CREAO is for them.
You describe what you need. The Super Agent delivers. You save what works. It runs on your schedule, with your tools, connected to your systems, getting better every conversation.
Chat. Create. Run it all.
Start now →
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What exactly is the Super Agent?
A: The Super Agent is CREAO's central AI — the thing you talk to. It researches, writes, analyzes, generates files, runs code, connects to your tools, and delivers real artifacts in real time. Think of it as a sharp colleague who listens, understands what you need, and gets to work immediately. It also builds the Agents you create — analyzing your successful conversations and extracting them into reusable workflows.
Q: What can it actually produce?
A: Real work product. Interactive HTML pages, PDFs, spreadsheets, SVG graphics, data analyses, code that runs, video files, and images up to 4K resolution. Generated artifacts open in an interactive preview panel where you can click, test, and verify before downloading. These are deliverables, ready to use or share.
Q: What AI models power it?
A: You choose. Claude Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.6. Gemini 3.1 Pro, Gemini 3 Flash. GPT-5.4. Switch between them in any conversation — different tasks benefit from different strengths, and the choice is always yours.
Q: How do I create an Agent?
A: Have a conversation that produces great results. Tell it to create an agent . The Super Agent analyzes the session, extracts the workflow, and builds it into a reusable Agent with structured input forms, version control, and team sharing. Anyone can run it with different inputs. Every refinement creates a new version. Previous versions are preserved.
Q: How do Scheduled Runs work?
A: Agents run on a recurring schedule — daily, weekly, monthly, or custom cron expressions. Configure it once and the Agent delivers at the interval you set. You can manage schedules in natural language right from chat. Each run creates a session entry so you can review output, check status, and track timing.
Q: What tools does the Super Agent connect to?
A: CREAO connects to the tools where your work lives via Connectors — powered by MCP (Model Context Protocol). Gmail, Google Calendar, Sheets, Slack, GitHub, Google Ads, Google Analytics, Search Console, Linear, Asana, and more. New connectors spin up constantly. Setup is conversational — mention the service you want and the agent walks you through it in the chat.
Q: Can I connect my own APIs?
A: Yes. Secret Key Management lets you securely store API keys, tokens, and credentials. The Super Agent accesses them as environment variables inside its sandbox — encrypted at rest, never displayed after creation. Any service with an API becomes available. Your X/Twitter bearer token, your Stripe key, your Notion API, a database password, an internal endpoint. You bring the key. CREAO does the work.
Q: What are Skills?
A: Skills are modular capabilities that define what the Super Agent knows how to do. Built-in Skills include web search, image generation, PDF creation, data analysis, and web scraping. Custom Skills let you teach the agent your own workflows — install from any public GitHub repo, upload a ZIP, or describe what you want and the agent builds the Skill for you in the conversation. Every Skill you add expands the Super Agent's capability across all your conversations and Agents.
Q: How does Memory work?
A: The Super Agent remembers your preferences, decisions, project context, and key facts across every conversation — permanently. It saves context proactively and recalls it automatically. Memory is categorized (preferences, facts, decisions, entities) and searchable. Two tiers work together: cross-thread Memory persists durable knowledge forever, while thread-level notes preserve task-specific context during long conversations. The more you use CREAO, the more precisely it delivers.
Q: Is there a code execution environment?
A: Yes — a full Linux sandbox behind every conversation. The Super Agent installs packages, writes and executes code, manages files, and runs processes in a real runtime. Python, Node.js, data transformations, API integrations — all running in a proper environment. The sandbox persists between messages, auto-pauses when idle, and resumes in about a second. When you create an Agent, the entire runtime environment is captured so future runs start from the same snapshot.
Q: What are Workspaces?
A: Workspaces organize your conversations, Skills, Connectors, Agents, and files into separate environments. Different projects get different configurations. The Super Agent's available capabilities depend on what's enabled in the active Workspace, keeping context clean. Agents can be assigned across multiple Workspaces, so a single well-built Agent serves different teams and contexts.
Q: What does AI-First actually mean?
A: AI-First is an operating approach. It's the shift from using AI for individual tasks to directing AI systems that run your work. CREAO is built on this conviction — and built this way internally. The team rebuilt the platform with AI as part of the development process. What we've learned is that with AI-First, judgment becomes the job — what to build, what to run, what to direct next. The tools handle the rest."
