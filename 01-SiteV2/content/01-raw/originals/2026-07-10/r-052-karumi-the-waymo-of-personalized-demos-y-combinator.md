TL;DR: Skip the reading! Karumi delivers personalized product demos, and it’s faster if our agent just shows you! → Demo now
Hi all! We are Toni and Pablo , and we are proud to launch Karumi .
Most companies still demo like it’s 1995 . Be honest: when was the last time you found someone available right now to give you a live demo?
Users don’t want to wait; they want to see how your product makes their lives easier.
That’s why we built Karumi, an AI agent that delivers personalized demos, 24/7 , and in any language .
https://youtu.be/pAgt4iZMOGs
Forget pre-recorded videos. Karumi gives your users a real demo experience - a live, interactive video call with an AI agent that:
Matches human reps in clarity and engagement.
Is always available , regardless of timezone.
Has access to a browser (it opens tabs, navigates your product, etc.)
Is hyper‑personalized , pulling in every piece of contextual data (prospect profile, product knowledge) to tailor the conversation.
Auto‑trains on your product and business changes, staying up‑to‑date without manual input.
You can also see it in live in Deel’s platform here:
https://www.loom.com/share/e7f7e00f2284478e8335f8f4d4dac6bd
Why did we build Karumi?
As SaaS companies scale, inbound leads grow quickly . Demo requests pour in, and human reps can only handle so many before leads start slipping through the cracks. The result:
❌ High-intent leads wait days for a personalized demo.
❌ Smaller accounts go unserved .
❌ Reps spend time chasing the wrong fits instead of closing deals.
❌ Quantity of demos takes priority, leaving quality and personalization behind .
Traditional sales motions don’t scale linearly with demand. Every new lead either adds friction or requires more headcount, creating bottlenecks and lost opportunities.
Why does it matter?
Every minute of delay between interest and engagement means lost revenue , especially in today’s crowded software landscape. Karumi closes that gap, giving every lead personalized attention at the moment of highest intent, including those who are currently underserved.
Our customers use Karumi to:
✅ Improve conversion rates from landing page visits to meeting s .
✅ Serve segments that previously went unserved .
✅ Shorten sales cycles by combining discovery, qualification & demonstration steps.
✅ Capture inbound demand during product launches or marketing surges.
