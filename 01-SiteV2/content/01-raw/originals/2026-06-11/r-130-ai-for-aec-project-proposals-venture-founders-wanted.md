# AI for AEC Project Proposals Venture - Founders Wanted

## clean_text

FutureSight is seeking an experienced and visionary Founding CEO to lead the launch of a new AI-native platform that transforms how engineering and construction firms win project proposals.
The problem is simple - but the consequences are significant.
Architecture, engineering, and construction, or (AEC) proposal and marketing teams spend weeks chasing technical experts for inputs, searching through past project plans and outdated content, and manually tracking compliance across complex RFQs and RFPs.. all under immovable deadlines.
One missing requirement. One outdated qualification. One compliance gap. And millions of dollars in project revenue can be lost.
The result is slower submissions, burned-out teams, and inconsistent win rates in an industry where predictable backlog directly determines the firm’s growth.
This problem is exciting for us because it’s pure execution.
High-stakes deadlines. Repetitive requirements. Multi-department coordination. Clear pass-fail outcomes in a multi-trillion-dollar global AEC project market
Our belief is straightforward: this is exactly the kind of work AI should own.
Tracking requirements, orchestrating inputs, and surfacing gaps.. so proposal leaders in AEC can focus on strategy, positioning, and telling a winning story rather than managing administrative work behind the scenes.
As the Founding CEO, you will:
Win customers by running pilots, shaping pricing, and validating retention + expansion.
Work with FutureSight’s product and engineering pod to launch your V1.
Hire your founding team and set the cultural foundation. And Lead the seed round with FutureSight’s initial investment.
What FutureSight brings to the table:
We have validated the problem and the space — You will enter this venture with a proven market need.
We have studio bench strength including designers, engineers, growth marketers, and fundraising pros who are embedded from Day 1.
We bring capital commitment with initial pre-seed funding for early hires and building the MVP with potential for follow-on funding, plus access to our investor and advisor network.
If you are as excited as we are about solving this problem, we want to hear from you. Feel free to read through our FAQs about working in FutureSight’s venture studio, watch “ What We Look for in an EIR ”, or Apply to this opportunity.
