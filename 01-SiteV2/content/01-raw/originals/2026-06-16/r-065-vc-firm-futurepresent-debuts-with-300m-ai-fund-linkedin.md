BREAKING: New VC futurepresent has come out of stealth with a debut fund of $300m to back AI companies!
The firm is focused on AI platforms and infrastructure across the US and Europe and has already made 14 investments.
It's deploying a barbell investing strategy that includes pre-seed/seed as well as concentrated growth investments. Investment to date include:
🚀 General Intuition
🚀 Skalar
🚀 Isidor
The partners are:
🔥 Thomas Lueke, former Partner at Cherry Ventures
🔥 Jan Rettel , former hedge fund manager investing in public tech
🔥 David Meiborg , former Partner at First Momentum Ventures
🔥 Johnson Yang , former Partner at General Catalyst
And Venture Partner Remy A. Lazarovici , who was employee #1 at Celonis.
