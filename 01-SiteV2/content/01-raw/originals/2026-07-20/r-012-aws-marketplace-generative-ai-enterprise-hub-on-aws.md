Enterprise Hub offers Application developers with a set of distinct yet interoperable components to solve for the various challenges they encounter through the lifecycle of their Generative AI project, such as data preparation, knowledge base construction, architecture bootstrapping, ongoing experimentation, evaluation and monitoring of their Generative AI application.

Enterprises today need a standardized way to accelerate deployment of Generative AI use cases into production with embedded security, financial, and policy compliance. Generative AI Enterprise Hub on AWS allows organizations to fast-track their generative AI projects while eliminating redundancy, bringing forth architecture standardization, and compliance.
Gen AI Enterprise Hub provides a suite of accelerators to bootstrap generative AI application development that customers can offer internally via a self-service experience providing teams across the organization with the ability to quickly setup generative AI architectures such as RAG, or Agentic workflow orchestration in a standardized way.
Gen AI Enterprise Hub standardizes how generative AI applications across the organization use LLMs by providing a Model Gateway that centralizes access to LLMs across various providers. Further, the Model Gateway allows customers to define and enforce compliance across all their generative AI applications offering capabilities such as centralized usage and cost controls, a consolidated way to configure guardrails that enforce compliance to responsible AI principles.
