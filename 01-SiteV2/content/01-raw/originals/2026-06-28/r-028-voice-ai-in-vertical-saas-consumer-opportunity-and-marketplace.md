# Voice AI in Vertical SaaS: Consumer Opportunity and Marketplace ...

## clean_text

Three years ago we advocated that “ Consumer Extension is the hero’s path in vertical SaaS! ” - few have succeeded, but the payoff is enormous for your merchant customers ( incremental demand and revenues) and you the Vertical SaaS (10-30% take rates and the love and gratitude of your merchant customers).
I believe that Voice AI will bring together consumer and vertical SaaS faster than expected. Consumer apps will reach out to small business merchants via voice agents to book reservations, check on status, all the little things that we hate to do by phone. Your merchants will be forced to field this new inbound or lose out valuable customers and revenues. See what showed up in my recent Capitola brunch search:
If this comes to be, Voice AI will set VSaaS and marketplaces on a collision course creating opportunity and threat.
Opportunity
Voice agents will reduce the friction of calling and create new categories of inquiry, call volumes will spike. Your merchants will be overwhelmed by inbound voice agents and will need you to help them.
If you own the consumer surfaces (see table below), you have an unfair right to offer an AI receptionist, central reservation system, etc. Structured correctly, you will also be able to pull the mobile numbers of the inquiring customers which will put you on a path of CRM greatness- inbound personalization, upsell and loyalty; Outbound renewal or look-alike acquisition campaigns. Now you’re into the world of customer acquisition and the 10-30% take rates that it brings…

Threat
If you don’t own the consumer surfaces, well you’ve just opened the door to the most formidable “integrate and surround” challenger.
It’s only natural for a marketplace to offer a merchant side agent to field these requests. They are bringing the customer demand, and their system can provide seamless workflows. With their 10-30% take rates, they can afford to offer this merchant side software for free. Their path from there is clear- first it’s inbound voice agents, then online booking, loyalty/ repeat campaigns, social media review management, price optimization, ad buying… Then they are ready to take a shot at the king–in-store point of sale…
Generative AI Coming Soon!
Generative AI is changing how businesses and consumers (employees & suppliers) communicate. Voice AI is just the start. Are you ready?
We’re working on two essays exploring the race between control point incumbent and Native AI challengers, as well as the convergence/ collision between Vertical SaaS and Consumer in much more detail.
