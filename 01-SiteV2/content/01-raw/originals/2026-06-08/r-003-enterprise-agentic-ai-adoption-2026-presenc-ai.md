Agent-native startups (Sierra, Decagon, Cresta, Cognigy, AI21) collectively reached approximately $1.5 billion in combined ARR by May 2026, primarily in customer experience and contact centre verticals.

Strategic Context
Three structural patterns define the 2026 enterprise agentic AI market. First, hyperscaler concentration: Microsoft, Salesforce, ServiceNow, and Google have captured most of the lighthouse enterprise deployments because their existing application footprint provides distribution that agent-native startups must work to match. Second, governance and identity have become procurement gate items: enterprises now require workload-identity, audit logging, and DLP integration before approving large agent deployments, which favours vendors with mature compliance stacks. Third, the CX vertical is consolidating fastest: customer experience agents are the highest-volume agent category, and a handful of agent-native players (Sierra, Decagon, Cresta) plus the application incumbents have collectively captured most of the deployments.
