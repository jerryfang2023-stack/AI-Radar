Pre-seed funding for AI startups dropped significantly in 2025. The data tells the story. 👇
Deal flow is down ~30% YoY, money raised down ~40% YoY. Average dilution at pre-seed down from a high of 44% in August 2024 to a low of 18% in August 2025.
Investors are backing fewer companies, but the right ones get better terms.
What changed:
2024: AI product + pitch deck = funding
2025: Technical moat + proven team + verifiable traction = funding
It's a founder's market if you have real differentiation. For everyone else, the bar is now much higher.
