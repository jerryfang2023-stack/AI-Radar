💰 Continuing my AWS journey — today's topic: cost management.
AWS has 4 built-in tools to monitor, analyze, and optimize your cloud spending.
Knowing which tool to use — and when — can save you from a very unpleasant billing surprise.
1. Cost Explorer
Visualize and analyze your historical usage and spending patterns across services, accounts, and time periods.
2. AWS Budgets
Set custom cost thresholds and receive alerts before your spending exceeds a defined limit.
3. AWS Compute Optimizer
Get machine learning–based recommendations for right-sizing your resources — so you're not paying for capacity you don't need.
4. AWS Pricing Calculator
Estimate the cost of your architecture before you deploy — great for planning and budgeting new workloads.
Cloud cost management isn't just about cutting costs — it's about spending wisely and knowing exactly where every dollar goes.
