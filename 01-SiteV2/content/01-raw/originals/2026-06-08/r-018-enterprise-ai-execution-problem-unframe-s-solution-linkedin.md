Enterprise AI has an execution problem - not a technology problem.

That gap is what Shay Levi pitched me when we first met. Not another platform, not another copilot — a managed delivery engine that turns enterprise use cases into tailored, production-grade AI solutions, in weeks. ⚡ He laid out his vision for where enterprise AI was headed, and it clicked immediately.

Today, Unframe announced a $50M Series B 🚀 led by Highland Capital Partners. The speed at which they've scaled is a reflection of how badly enterprises need this — and of a founding team, Larissa Schneider, Adi Azarya, and Shay Levi that executes with rare intensity.
