BREAKING: UK AI startup Archestra.AI has raised a $10m seed led by 20VC !
The company, founded by serial entrepreneurs Matvey Kukuy and Ildar Iskhakov , has built an open-source platform that enables enterprises to connect sensitive data to AI agents securely.
The problem they are tackling is that, to date, companies have had to choose between locking their agents down so tight that they're useless, or letting them loose and praying there are no leaks.
Since launch, the platform has grown to more than 3,700 GitHub stars, 57 contributors, and deployments across several Fortune 50 environments.
Today the company has announced raising a $10m Seed Round led by 20VC !
It's got a long list of other prominent backers such as Will Wu , Olivier Pomel , Visible Ventures ( Carolyn Everson ), Tenacity Capital ( AJ Tennant ) and many others.
