Kore.ai announces the availability of its AI for work, AI for process, and AI for service in the new AWS marketplace AI agents and tools category
July 27, 2025
ORLANDO, FL – July 17th – Kore.ai, a global leader in enterprise AI technology, today announced the availability of its AI for Work, AI for Process and AI for Service in the new AI Agents and Tools category of AWS Marketplace. Customers can now use AWS Marketplace to easily discover, buy, and deploy AI Agents solutions, including Kore.ai ’s business solutions using their AWS accounts, accelerating AI agent and agentic workflow development. ‍
Kore.ai prebuilt business solutions and industry solutions (for banking, healthcare, retail, travel) help organizations to empower every employee work faster and smarter with powerful enterprise search and prebuilt agents, elevate their customer service through AI assistance to customers and service reps, and improve operational efficiency and business outcomes by transforming knowledge-intensive operations.
“By offering Kore.ai products through the AWS Marketplace, we’re providing customers with a streamlined way to access our agentic platform and business solutions, helping them buy and deploy AI agent solutions faster and more efficiently,” said Raj Koneru, CEO and Founder at Kore.ai. “Our customers in BFSI, healthcare, retail and travel industries are already using these capabilities to achieve improved efficiency and speed of business, demonstrating the real-world value. We are also closely working with the AWS team to integrate our AI agents across the stack with Amazon models, GenAI infrastructure, and their platforms such as Amazon Q, Amazon Connect, and Bedrock.”
With the availability of AI Agents and Tools in AWS Marketplace, customers can significantly accelerate their procurement process to drive AI innovation, reducing the time needed for vendor evaluations and complex negotiations. With centralized purchasing using AWS accounts, customers maintain visibility and control over licensing, payments, and access through AWS.
Kore.ai AI for Work, AI for Process and AI for Service deliver essential capabilities including prebuilt agents and templates, infrastructure and models-agnostic architecture, sophisticated agentic capabilities, deep integrations, and security and compliance. These features enable customers to quickly deploy AI for their specific use cases at scale.
Available as a SaaS solution, Kore.ai products support model context protocol (MCP) and Agent2Agent (A2A) for streamlined agent communication. This enables customers to seamlessly connect with other agents and AWS services or flexibly deploy across their AWS environment.
To learn more about Kore.ai products in AWS Marketplace, visit AWS Marketplace listing . To learn more about the new AI Agents and Tools storefront in AWS Marketplace,
