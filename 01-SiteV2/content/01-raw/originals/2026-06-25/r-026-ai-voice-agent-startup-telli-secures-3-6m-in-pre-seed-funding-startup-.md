Global
Share Share Share Pin
News Summary
Telli, a Berlin-based startup and Y Combinator alum, has successfully raised $3.6 million in a pre-seed funding round led by Cherry Ventures and Y Combinator, with additional support from angel investors. The company specializes in AI voice agents designed to streamline high-volume customer interactions by automating tasks such as appointment bookings, lead prequalification, and product suggestions. Founded by Seb Hapte-Selassie, Philipp Baumanns, and Finn zur Mühlen, Telli’s technology utilizes voice cloning platforms and various AI models to deliver effective solutions tailored to customer needs. The startup has reported over 50% monthly revenue growth and has processed nearly a million calls. With its new funding, Telli plans to expand its operations further across Germany, the U.K., Latin America, and the U.S.
