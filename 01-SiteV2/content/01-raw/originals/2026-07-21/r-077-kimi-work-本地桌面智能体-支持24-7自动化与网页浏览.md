What's the difference between Kimi Work and the Web version?
While the Kimi web app is perfect for quick chat and queries, Kimi Work is a Local Agent designed for deep workflows. It mounts your local folders, navigates the web autonomously via WebBridge, runs Python code in the background, and executes scheduled tasks. It's a system-level digital employee.
How does Kimi Work protect my privacy when accessing local files?
You have absolute control over your files. The built-in Ask before acting safeguard means Kimi will prompt you for explicit authorization before it modifies, overwrites, or runs code within your local directories. Nothing happens without your consent.
What exactly can WebBridge (Browser Automation) do for me?
WebBridge gives Kimi the ability to use a browser like a human. You can tell it to check the latest news on a website and summarize it, or scrape historical stock data to your local Excel. It clicks, scrolls, and extracts data autonomously, saving you hours of manual work.
