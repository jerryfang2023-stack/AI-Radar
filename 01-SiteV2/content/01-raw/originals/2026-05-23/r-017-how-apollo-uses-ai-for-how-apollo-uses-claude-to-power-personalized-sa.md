Full Story
Sales teams face a persistent tension between personalization and scale: truly tailored outreach converts far better, but writing individualized messages for hundreds of prospects is impractical. Apollo, a leading sales engagement platform, recognized that AI could bridge this gap — but only if it was accessible to sellers who lack the time or inclination to configure complex AI systems.
The challenge was making powerful AI capabilities feel seamless rather than technical. As Apollo's AI Product Lead noted, the technology can be very powerful but is not accessible to users who want results without extensive setup. Apollo needed a model that could produce genuinely compelling sales copy while integrating smoothly into existing workflows.
Apollo integrated Claude 3.5 Haiku into its platform to power three core capabilities: personalized email and messaging generation, intelligent prospect research, and lead discovery. Claude drafts outreach that reads as authentically human while incorporating relevant context about the prospect and their company. In blind testing, users preferred Claude-generated messages 76% of the time over alternatives.
The results across Apollo's customer base have been measurable and consistent. Customers using Claude-powered messaging report a 35% increase in meeting bookings and a 15% improvement in retention rates. Platform-wide, Apollo now processes more than 5 million Claude-powered messaging actions every month. Individual customers have reported even sharper gains: one saw email writing time drop by 80%, while another cut prospecting time by 40%.
