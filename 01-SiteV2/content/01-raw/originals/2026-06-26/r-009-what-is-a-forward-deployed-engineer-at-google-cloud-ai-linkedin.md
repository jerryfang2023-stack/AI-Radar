# What is a Forward Deployed Engineer at Google Cloud AI? - LinkedIn

How We Think About Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) at Google Cloud Applied AI
How We Think About Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) at Google Cloud Applied AI
We're one of the first teams at Google to officially adopt the title of Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE). It's a relatively new role at Google Cloud and in the industry by and large. People often ask me what the role really is. It's actually not that difficult to understand if you break it down into the following dimensions:
The Job Ladder: All of us are on the traditional Software Engineering (SWE) ladder. But customer-facing competency is a must-have.
Generalist vs. Specialist: Unlike solution architects who have experience in a wide range of areas, we are product specialists. We understand the intricate details of our AI voice agent product and can debug the most challenging production issues. While we're familiar with general Google Cloud offerings, we defer to other SMEs for areas outside our specific product.
Scope of Customer Commitment: In contrast to sales engineers or customer engineers, who are dedicated to see through the implementation of a single customer (and often earn commissions), our engagements are more episodic. They're scoped to specific topics, such as building a demo, debugging a performance issue, or improving AI quality for a particular use case. Our involvement has varying durations/scopes, driven by the strategic needs of the product.
Customer Contract: Unlike professional service organizations or ISVs, our customers don't sign a "FDE contract" and don't directly pay for our work. Sometimes, we don't even differentiate the support from FDE vs the core eng team in front of customers.
Consultation vs. Hands-On Coding: We primarily focus on hands-on coding—though in our world, it's more about hands-on prompting.
Organization We Belong To: We are part of the engineering organization, which provides us with direct insights into the product's internal workings.
Our Goal: Ultimately, our goal is to drive product adoption and bring improvement feedback back to the product/eng team.
In short, we are a group of product specialist SWEs embedded within the engineering organization. We engage in episodic, hands-on coding for specific customer issues based on strategic demand, all without a formal service contract. Our aim is to drive product adoption and improvement for our AI voice agent portfolio.
