GlobalLogic Announces Availability of VelocityAI on Google Cloud Marketplace to Accelerate Enterprise AI Adoption and IT/OT Integration at Scale

Apr 22 2026 13:00 BST
New marketplace solutions enabled by Gemini Enterprise combine digital, enterprise, and physical AI to drive productivity, efficiency and business transformation
LAS VEGAS --(BUSINESS WIRE)--Apr. 22, 2026--
At Google Cloud Next 2026, GlobalLogic Inc. , a Hitachi Group Company and leader in digital engineering, today announced the availability of its VelocityAI suite of offerings, enabled by Gemini Enterprise application and Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform, on Google Cloud Marketplace .
This powerful synergy enables enterprises to move beyond fragmented AI pilots to scalable, production-grade AI deployments, aligned with Google Cloud’s AI technologies. GlobalLogic is also introducing two new offerings that will be available on Google Cloud Marketplace , Complex Enterprise Transformation and Physical AI & IT/OT Convergence, which support repeatable AI deployments across Manufacturing & Industrial, Financial Services, Retail & Consumer, Healthcare & Life Sciences and other industries.
“Our strategic partnership with Google Cloud , along with our ongoing investments in Gemini Enterprise, empowers enterprise clients to responsibly scale agentic and Physical AI solutions,” said Srini Shankar , President & CEO, GlobalLogic and CEO, Hitachi Digital Services . “VelocityAI, enabled by Google Cloud and Gemini, provides a unified intelligence layer that connects software, enterprise workflows and physical systems—enabling our clients to drive enterprise-wide transformation with measurable ROI.”
A 2025 MIT report illustrated that a significant majority of enterprise AI initiatives fail to scale beyond pilots due to lack of strategy, poor data governance, and failure to integrate into workflows. VelocityAI addresses these implementation challenges by harnessing the combined power of AI, digital engineering, and human-centered design to transform bold ideas into measurable business outcomes. By combining VelocityAI with Google Cloud’s infrastructure and Gemini Enterprise, Google's powerful AI operating system for enterprise data, businesses now have the unique ability to move from experimentation to enterprise-wide AI adoption with built-in security, compliance and scalability through three pillars:
Enterprise AI — Delivering intelligent workflows and products for operational efficiency
Physical AI — Bridging digital intelligence with real-world systems
AI-Powered SDLC — Accelerating the entire software development lifecycle
WebPT, a Warburg Pincus portfolio company and a leading rehab therapy platform, is partnering with GlobalLogic to re-imagine and reinvent themselves as an AI first business. From AI-native SDLC to agentic deployment at scale, WebPT and GlobalLogic are driving transformation at an accelerated pace to create a new paradigm for how vertical SaaS businesses navigate the new normal.
"By leveraging GlobalLogic's VelocityAI solutions on Google Cloud , our team is able to bring new, innovative offerings to our members so that they can provide the best outcomes for their patients,” said Andrea Facini , CEO, WebPT. “We are moving from isolated AI pilots to enterprise-wide deployment in a matter of weeks. Being able to innovate at this unprecedented rate is instrumental in accelerating our AI-led transformation journey to better support our customers."
Complex Enterprise Transformation delivered through VelocityAI's newly enhanced capabilities, enabled by Gemini Enterprise, allows for automation of complex business processes, legacy modernization and intelligent decision systems. GlobalLogic strategically deploys low-code agents to help transform human workflows. Combined with Gemini Enterprise and the data capabilities of Google Cloud , the company can broaden and improve intuitive communication between complex systems and a client’s human workforce, ensuring seamless human-in-the-loop efficiency as well as measurable productivity and efficiency gains.
Physical AI &
IT/OT Convergence further showcases VelocityAI’s power, leveraging GlobalLogic and Hitachi’s IT/OT heritage and the Google Cloud environment, to bridge digital AI with physical assets, unlocking new revenue streams and operational efficiencies. By deploying VelocityAI’s physical AI integrations with Gemini Enterprise, companies can leverage live sensor intelligence and interact directly with industrial assets. This transforms Gemini into a frontline operational commander, allowing for greater control, predictive maintenance, and optimized physical operations.
“Bringing GlobalLogic to Google Cloud Marketplace will help customers quickly deploy, manage, and grow VelocityAI on Google Cloud's trusted, global infrastructure," said Dai Vu , Managing Director, Marketplace & ISV GTM Programs at Google Cloud . “GlobalLogic can now securely scale and support customers on their digital transformation journeys.”
This announcement builds upon the multiyear collaboration between Hitachi — GlobalLogic’s parent company — and Google Cloud . The relationship has deepened significantly since the partnership’s inception, including GlobalLogic’s investment to become a Gemini Enterprise Launch Partner in 2025 , which further solidified its role in bringing enterprise-grade AI solutions to market.
For more information, visit GlobalLogic at Google Cloud Next, Booth 2207, April 22-24 .
About GlobalLogic
GlobalLogic , a Hitachi Group Company , is a leading digital engineering partner that helps the world’s most forward-thinking companies design and build innovative, AI-powered products, platforms, and digital experiences. Since 2000, we’ve been at the forefront of the digital revolution, now accelerating clients’ transitions into tomorrow’s AI-driven businesses by integrating experience design, complex engineering, AI, and data expertise. Headquartered in Silicon Valley , GlobalLogic is a Hitachi Group Company operating under Hitachi, Ltd. (TSE: 6501), which contributes to a sustainable society with a higher quality of life by driving innovation through AI and technology as the Social Innovation Business.
