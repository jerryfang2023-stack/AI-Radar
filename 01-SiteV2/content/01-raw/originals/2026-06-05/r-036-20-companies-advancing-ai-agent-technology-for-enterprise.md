From autonomous workflows to intelligent copilots embedded across enterprise systems, AI agents are rapidly transforming how organizations operate, optimize decisions, and empower teams. Driven by cost pressures, talent shortages, and the need to accelerate digital transformation, agentic systems have become one of the most strategically important technologies across industries.
FounderNest analyzed 354 companies innovating across this emerging space, collectively raising $228.29 billion in publicly disclosed funding as of November 2025.
These companies span model providers, orchestration platforms, workflow automation startups, document-intelligence engines, and enterprise AI assistants, building the next generation of cognitive automation.
Download the market report
Key market trends
Intelligent Process Automation (IPA)
One of the strongest “Hot Tech” categories, where AI agents orchestrate complex, multi-step processes across enterprise systems, from finance and HR to supply chain and operations.
AI-powered Workforce Productivity Tools
AI copilots and autonomous assistants increasingly augment employees, automating scheduling, task management, research, and internal queries.
Predictive Analytics and Decision Support
AI agents embed real-time analytical reasoning into operations, anticipating issues, mitigating risk, and enabling data-driven, autonomous decision-making.
AI-driven Cybersecurity
Agentic systems that monitor threats, analyze anomalies, and autonomously respond to vulnerabilities across cloud and enterprise environments.
NLP for Customer and Employee Support Automation
Conversational agents streamline internal support, IT service desks, and customer ticketing workflows, reducing workload and response times.
Low-code / No-code AI Development
Platforms enabling enterprises to build, deploy, and manage AI agents without deep machine learning expertise.
AI for Supply Chain Optimization
Agents that synthesize internal and external signals to optimize forecasting, procurement, routing, and inventory.
