1/ MeltPlan: $14M Seed

4/ Patientdesk.ai (YC W26): $1M Pre-Seed
AI that answers dental front desks 24/7, books appointments, verifies insurance in real-time, and plugs directly into existing practice management software. YC-backed, SF-based.
5/ RLWRLD: $26M Seed
They are training robotics foundation models inside real, messy factories and warehouses. Headline Asia, Kakao Investment, and a deep roster of Korean strategics backed it.
6/ ValkaAI: $14M Pre-Seed
Prague-based ValkaAI is building AI sports commentators that react to live events in real-time. The pitch is turning passive broadcasts into personalized, interactive experiences. Rockaway Ventures led.
7/ oska health: $13M Seed
Frankfurt-based Oska Health is building a hybrid care model tackling kidney disease, diabetes, and hypertension between doctor visits. Human health coaches work alongside AI to handle everything from nutrition to medication adherence. Capricorn Partners and SwissHealth Ventures led.
8/ Callosum: $10M Pre-Seed
London-based Callosum lets AI models run across whatever mix of chips a company has, instead of locking them into massive GPU clusters. The approach schedules workloads across heterogeneous hardware automatically. Plural led, with ARIA and Charlie Songhurst joining.
9/ General Magic: $7M Seed
They run insurance workflows entirely over SMS and iMessage. Its AI agents handle everything from pre-quote eligibility to claims coordination, plugging into existing broker systems. Radical Ventures led, with a16z Speedrun and Aidan Gomez backing it out of Toronto.
10/ Kinfolk: $7M Seed
They give HR teams AI agents that actually do the work inside Slack and Teams. Updating HRIS records, drafting documents, managing lifecycle changes, etc. AlbionVC led, London-based.
