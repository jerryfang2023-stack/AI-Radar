Executive Summary
Retrofit-Recommender targets the $12B industrial maintenance software market with a differentiated AI-sustainability positioning. Our go-to-market strategy combines product-led growth (freemium tier for awareness) with enterprise direct sales (high-touch for $50K+ deals) and partner channels (sustainability consultancies as resellers).
12-Month Goal: $2M ARR, 50+ paying customers, 10,000 tons CO2e avoided
