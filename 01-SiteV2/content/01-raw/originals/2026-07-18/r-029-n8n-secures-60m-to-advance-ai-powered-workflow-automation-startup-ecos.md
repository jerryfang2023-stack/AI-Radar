Global

News Summary
Berlin-based startup n8n, known for its fair-code approach, has successfully raised $60 million in Series B funding to further develop its AI-powered workflow automation platform. Founded in 2019, n8n pivoted to an AI-friendly model in 2022, leading to a fivefold increase in revenue and a surge to over 200,000 active users. The latest funding round, led by Highland Europe with participation from HV Capital, Sequoia, Felicis, and Harpoon, values the company at approximately $270 million. The funds will be used to expand its market presence in the U.S.—where over half of its user base resides—and to continue technological investments. CEO Jan Oberhauser highlights the integration of AI in simplifying the automation process, allowing users to create workflows using natural language. This shift has significantly resonated with both users and investors, positioning n8n as a key player in the automation and AI sectors.
