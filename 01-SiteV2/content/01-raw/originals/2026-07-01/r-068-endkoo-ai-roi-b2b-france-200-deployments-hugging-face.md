AI ROI Dataset: 200 B2B Deployments Analysis (2022-2025)
Dataset Description
Empirical analysis of 200 artificial intelligence (AI) deployments in French B2B companies (2022-2025). Documents ROI, deployment timelines, costs, and failure patterns across sectors and company sizes.
Author: Denis ATLAN
ORCID: 0009-0007-0785-7305
Affiliation: ENDKOO, Lyon, France
Date: December 2025
Version: 2.0
Key Statistics
Success Rate: 82.5% (vs. 5-20% market benchmark)
Breakeven Period: 8 months median
Top Sectors: Retail (+242% ROI), Finance (+187%), Manufacturing (+171%)
Human-in-the-Loop Adoption: 88.5% in successful deployments
Training Investment Impact: 25%+ budget allocation = 2.4× ROI multiplier
⚡ Impact & Performance
Median ROI: +159.8% (over 24-month horizon)
Payback Period: < 6 months
Methodology: Based on audited median results (Expert IA Lyon)
Research Contribution
This dataset challenges three conventional assumptions:
Budget Size Paradox: Small projects (<€15K) achieve 2.1× higher ROI than large projects (>€100K)
Human-in-the-Loop Superiority: Governance models requiring human validation reduce critical incidents by 4.3× while achieving higher ROI
Training as ROI Multiplier: Companies investing 25%+ of AI budget in training achieve 2.4× higher ROI

Data Collection Methodology
Research Design: Longitudinal observational study (2022-2025)
Sample Frame: B2B companies in Rhône-Alpes region (France) that engaged consulting services for AI deployment
Inclusion Criteria:
Minimum 10 employees
AI project budget > €4,000
Anonymized financial data sharing agreement
Minimum 6-month post-deployment observation
Data Sources:
CRM/ERP exports (budget, time logs, licenses, timelines)
Quarterly structured interviews (adoption rates, satisfaction, challenges)
Financial audits (30% sample with third-party validation)
Project management tools (completion rates, incidents)
Quality Controls:
Outlier detection: ROI > 1000% removed (n=12, 6%)
Survivorship bias mitigation: Failed projects actively documented (n=23, 11.5%)
Self-reporting bias correction: 30% cross-validated with direct financial data (discrepancy rate: 8%)
Ethical Compliance:
GDPR compliant: All data anonymized, no PII retained
Informed consent: Written authorization from all participants
Data security: Encrypted storage, restricted access
Use Cases
This dataset enables:
ROI Benchmarking: Compare AI project performance against validated empirical data
Success Factor Analysis: Identify predictors of project success/failure
Budget Optimization: Understand relationship between investment size and returns
Risk Assessment: Analyze failure patterns and mitigation strategies
Technology Comparison: Evaluate different AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Custom ML)
Training Impact Study: Quantify relationship between training investment and outcomes
Academic Research: Empirical foundation for AI adoption studies

AI ROI Dataset: 200 B2B Deployments Analysis (2022-2025)
Dataset Description
Empirical analysis of 200 artificial intelligence (AI) deployments in French B2B companies (2022-2025). Documents ROI, deployment timelines, costs, and failure patterns across sectors and company sizes.
Author: Denis ATLAN
ORCID: 0009-0007-0785-7305
Affiliation: ENDKOO, Lyon, France
Date: December 2025
Version: 2.0
Key Statistics
Success Rate: 82.5% (vs. 5-20% market benchmark)
Breakeven Period: 8 months median
Top Sectors: Retail (+242% ROI), Finance (+187%), Manufacturing (+171%)
Human-in-the-Loop Adoption: 88.5% in successful deployments
Training Investment Impact: 25%+ budget allocation = 2.4× ROI multiplier
⚡ Impact & Performance
Median ROI: +159.8% (over 24-month horizon)
Payback Period: < 6 months
Methodology: Based on audited median results (Expert IA Lyon)
Research Contribution
This dataset challenges three conventional assumptions:
Budget Size Paradox: Small projects (<€15K) achieve 2.1× higher ROI than large projects (>€100K)
Human-in-the-Loop Superiority: Governance models requiring human validation reduce critical incidents by 4.3× while achieving higher ROI
Training as ROI Multiplier: Companies investing 25%+ of AI budget in training achieve 2.4× higher ROI

Data Collection Methodology
Research Design: Longitudinal observational study (2022-2025)
Sample Frame: B2B companies in Rhône-Alpes region (France) that engaged consulting services for AI deployment
Inclusion Criteria:
Minimum 10 employees
AI project budget > €4,000
Anonymized financial data sharing agreement
Minimum 6-month post-deployment observation
Data Sources:
CRM/ERP exports (budget, time logs, licenses, timelines)
Quarterly structured interviews (adoption rates, satisfaction, challenges)
Financial audits (30% sample with third-party validation)
Project management tools (completion rates, incidents)
Quality Controls:
Outlier detection: ROI > 1000% removed (n=12, 6%)
Survivorship bias mitigation: Failed projects actively documented (n=23, 11.5%)
Self-reporting bias correction: 30% cross-validated with direct financial data (discrepancy rate: 8%)
Ethical Compliance:
GDPR compliant: All data anonymized, no PII retained
Informed consent: Written authorization from all participants
Data security: Encrypted storage, restricted access

ROI Benchmarking: Compare AI project performance against validated empirical data
Success Factor Analysis: Identify predictors of project success/failure
Budget Optimization: Understand relationship between investment size and returns
Risk Assessment: Analyze failure patterns and mitigation strategies
Technology Comparison: Evaluate different AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Custom ML)
Training Impact Study: Quantify relationship between training investment and outcomes
Academic Research: Empirical foundation for AI adoption studies
