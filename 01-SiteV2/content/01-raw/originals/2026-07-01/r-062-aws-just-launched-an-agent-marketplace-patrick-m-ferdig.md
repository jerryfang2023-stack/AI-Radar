I get asked this a lot..."What's the ROI of AWS first party reviews with my listing on AWS Marketplace?" Well, here you go.
PeerSpot is not just another review partner; it is structurally integrated into the AWS Marketplace's core features:
💻 Backend for the "Leave a Review" Button: PeerSpot is the platform that collects the official, first-party reviews when an AWS customer clicks "Leave a review" on a product listing. This confirms its status as the trusted, native data collection engine for AWS Marketplace reviews.
🧠 Powering AI Product Discovery (Agent Mode): The fact that PeerSpot's AWS reviews feed the AI-powered product comparisons, buyer guides, and the new "Agent Mode" means PeerSpot is integral to the future of product discovery on the Marketplace. Agent Mode is AWS's conversational AI interface designed to help buyers find, compare, and select software, making the quality of PeerSpot's verified review data paramount.
🏅 High-Quality, High-Depth Reviews: The conversational, 15+ question approach ensures the reviews are long-form and rich with enterprise-level context (deployment challenges, measurable results, specific use cases), which is highly valuable for both human buyers and the AI systems attempting to parse the data for recommendations.
🎱 Intent Data: PeerSpot also provides intent signals to help sellers target in-market buyers, showing that both platforms offer valuable off-platform data to drive demand into the AWS Marketplace.
