# actAVA is live on the AWS Marketplace! | actAVA Blog

## clean_text

actAVA is now available on the AWS Marketplace. That is a three-word sentence that carries a lot of practical weight for healthcare and enterprise technology leaders who have been watching the agentic AI space and wondering when the procurement question would get easier. Today it did.
The actAVA Agentic Platform listing on AWS Marketplace includes the full actAVA platform &mdash; KORA (our agentic development lifecycle suite), CHRYSO (our agentic governance suite), and &chi;-BENCH (our healthcare benchmark) &mdash; available for purchase through the same consolidated procurement channel AWS customers use for infrastructure, security, analytics, and every other enterprise technology purchase. Three tiers. Flexible contracting. Forward-deployed engineering support included. And all of it counted toward your AWS committed spend.
Here is what this means, what the platform includes, and what AWS customers should know about getting started.
WHY THIS MATTERS
The Procurement Barrier Was the Last Mile Problem in Enterprise AI Adoption
The organizations that have been slowest to move from AI pilots to production deployment are often not the ones with the weakest technical teams or the least compelling use cases. They are the ones where every new AI vendor requires a separate procurement process: a new legal review, a new security assessment, a new vendor onboarding sequence, a new contract vehicle that finance has to approve, and a separate billing relationship that does not fit neatly into existing cost structures.
AWS Marketplace removes that entire layer for AWS customers. actAVA is now a pre-approved vendor in the same procurement channel that healthcare systems, health plans, and enterprise technology teams already use for their other cloud infrastructure. One contract vehicle. One consolidated bill. One security and compliance framework that AWS customers have already accepted. The path from "we want to evaluate actAVA" to "we have a signed contract and are building agents" is now measured in days rather than quarters.
Simplified Procurement
No separate vendor onboarding, legal review cycle, or standalone contract vehicle. actAVA is available through the same AWS Marketplace procurement flow organizations already use for every other enterprise software purchase.
AWS Committed Spend
actAVA purchases count toward your AWS Enterprise Discount Program commitment. Organizations with EDP obligations can deploy the actAVA Agentic Platform and have it count toward committed spend; a meaningful benefit for enterprise AWS customers managing large annual commitments.
Consolidated Billing
actAVA charges appear in your existing AWS bill alongside your other cloud spend. Finance teams see a single vendor relationship, a single reporting line, and a single cost management framework across the full platform investment.
THE PLATFORM
Three Products. One Platform. The Full Agentic Lifecycle.
The actAVA Agentic Platform available on AWS Marketplace is not a single tool, but rather a complete system for building, evaluating, governing, and continuously improving AI agents across enterprise operations. It includes three integrated products that address the three phases in which agentic AI most often fails: building agents that actually work in production, governing them once they are in production, and measuring whether they are performing to the standard the workflow requires.
THE ACTAVA AGENTIC PLATFORM
KORA
Agentic development lifecycle suite. Build, deploy, and continuously improve multi-modal agents with governed orchestration, healthcare-native interoperability, and real-time ROI measurement.
CHRYSO
Agentic governance suite. Real-time monitoring, automated control evidence, and compliance against NIST AI RMF, HIPAA, CMS HEI, and ONC HT1 with policy gap analysis and approve/revise/reject decisions.
&chi;-BENCH
Healthcare benchmark. The first evaluation framework that tests agentic AI in healthcare administration, as it actually runs prior authorization, UM, and care management, is grounded in real-world policy and operational workflows.
KORA
KORA: Build and Deploy Agents That Perform in Production
KORA is the core of the actAVA Agentic Platform, the system that takes an organization from "we want to deploy AI agents" to agents that are running, governed, improving, and generating measurable ROI across enterprise workflows.
It supports conversational, background, and voice AI agents across multi-step workflows, with human oversight built into critical decision points rather than bolted on as an afterthought. Healthcare-native interoperability connects agents to clinical and administrative systems without requiring custom integration for every connection. Before any agent reaches production, behavioral benchmarking validates its performance against pre-built and customer-defined standards. After deployment, multi-timescale learning turns real-world outcomes into auditable improvements, agents that get better continuously, without compromising the guardrails they were deployed with.
Utilization management and real-time ROI reporting give organizations visibility into what each agent costs to run and what it delivers, making the business case for each deployment a measurable, defensible number rather than an assumption from the pilot phase.
For business teams, not just engineering teams. KORA's natural-language, low-code agent builder enables business teams to build and launch agents from guided conversations rather than from code. Organizations do not have to centralize all agent development in engineering. The people closest to the workflow, the prior authorization team, the revenue cycle staff, and the care managers, can build and refine the agents that run their work.
CHRYSO
CHRYSO: Govern AI Agents at the Speed the Regulatory Environment Requires
The most common failure mode in enterprise AI governance is not the absence of a policy; it is the absence of a system for enforcing that policy continuously across a growing fleet of deployed agents. CHRYSO is that system.
CHRYSO provides real-time monitoring and automated control evidence against NIST AI RMF, HIPAA, CMS HEI, and ONC HT1, the regulatory frameworks that govern AI in healthcare and enterprise-regulated industries. It analyzes organizational policies against specific framework requirements, identifies critical gaps and missing controls, and assigns a completeness score from 0 to 100, along with actionable recommendations for immediate improvement.
Cross-framework validation means that an organization satisfying HIPAA requirements through CHRYSO is simultaneously building toward its NIST AI RMF posture &mdash; not running separate compliance processes for each framework. Every policy review results in a definitive decision: approve, revise, or reject, with documentation that survives an audit.
CHI-BENCH
&chi;-BENCH: Know What Your Agents Can Actually Do Before They Do It in Production
The most dangerous moment in healthcare AI deployment is not when an agent fails visibly &mdash; it is when it appears to work in a demo environment and fails in a production workflow. &chi;-BENCH was built to close that gap: the first benchmark specifically designed to evaluate agentic AI on healthcare administrative workflows as they actually run, not as they appear in controlled demonstrations.
Built and reviewed by AI, clinical, and academic experts, &chi;-BENCH evaluates complex workflows &mdash; prior authorization, utilization management, care management &mdash; across dense policy documents, hidden state, multi-role coordination, and provider-payer handoffs. Its tasks are grounded in real managed-care operations and constitute one of the largest and most realistic evaluations of agentic AI in a policy-dense enterprise setting.
The results consistently show a clear performance gap between frontier AI models and the reliability healthcare requires, which is precisely why the harness the agent runs on matters as much as the model in the Brain seat. &chi;-BENCH gives healthcare organizations the measurement infrastructure to know where that gap exists before they deploy, not after. Learn more at actava.ai/benchmarks .
WHAT THIS MEANS FOR AWS CUSTOMERS
Five Things AWS Customers Should Know
01 &mdash; Your EDP Commitment Covers This
If your organization has an AWS Enterprise Discount Program commitment, actAVA purchases on AWS Marketplace count toward that spend. Enterprise customers with large annual commitments can deploy actAVA to reduce their EDP balance, turning an agentic AI investment into committed spend burn-down.
02 &mdash; No New Vendor Onboarding Required
AWS Marketplace vendors have already cleared the procurement, legal, and security review processes your organization requires of new software vendors. actAVA is now in the approved channel. Your procurement team does not need to initiate a new vendor onboarding sequence &mdash; they need to initiate a Marketplace subscription.
03 &mdash; One Bill, One Cost Center
actAVA spend appears in your AWS consolidated bill alongside your existing infrastructure, storage, compute, and other SaaS purchases. Finance teams working from AWS Cost Explorer and Cost and Usage Reports will see actAVA as a line item in the same reporting framework they use for every other AWS investment.
04 &mdash; PNC Vendor Finance Is Available
For eligible US-based AWS customers, AWS Marketplace now accepts line-of-credit payments through the PNC Vendor Finance program. Organizations that want to spread the cost of an annual actAVA contract across the year, rather than paying upfront, have a financing option available directly through the Marketplace.
05 &mdash; AWS Agent Mode Supports Evaluation
The actAVA listing on AWS Marketplace supports AWS's new Agent Mode, enabling AI-assisted evaluation and requirements gathering directly within the Marketplace interface. Organizations that want to assess whether actAVA fits their requirements can use the Marketplace's own agentic evaluation tools to explore the listing before committing to a contract.
GET STARTED
The Path From Pilot to Production Just Got Shorter
The question for most healthcare and enterprise technology leaders is no longer whether agentic AI has a role in their operations. It does. The question is how quickly and safely they can move from evaluation to deployed agents that generate measurable value without introducing new compliance risk, creating new procurement overhead, or building on a foundation that cannot scale across the enterprise.
AVAILABLE NOW
The actAVA Agentic Platform is live on AWS Marketplace &mdash; three tiers, forward-deployed engineering included, counting toward your AWS committed spend.
The full KORA + CHRYSO + &chi;-BENCH platform, with forward-deployed engineering support included to get agents into production from day one. View listing for package details and pricing.
View on AWS Marketplace &rarr;
AWS customers who have been building their cloud infrastructure on the premise that healthcare AI would eventually be production-ready &mdash; at the compliance standard, at the governance level, and at the procurement simplicity their organizations require &mdash; now have a direct path to get there. actAVA is the AI factory.
