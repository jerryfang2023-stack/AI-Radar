# SaaS Evolution: AI-Enabled Platforms Drive Customer Value

## clean_text

"SaaS is dead." 💀
"SaaS is dead." 💀
I continue to hear and read this everywhere in this age of AI. After hundreds of data points - conversations with customers & industry peers and plenty of light reading - my personal POV is the opposite:
AI is the biggest opportunity on the table to further strengthen & mature SaaS, not replace it. (Note: For clarity, this is my POV on the overall industry and not specifically the company that employs me)
Today's SaaS platforms provide companies with trusted data, process workflows, and integrated & mission critical systems that organizations rely on to run their businesses. AI presents the monumental opportunity to build on that foundation - unlocking new levels of automation, insights, and intelligent decision-making. (And sure, a lot of other quick, fun things like this picture it made me in 15 seconds)
From my perspective, where this intersection becomes especially powerful is within industry verticals. This is what excites me the most as someone who has spent years partnering across industries focused on finding and enabling industry-specific value and process expertise.
When AI is combined with SaaS platforms and deep industry context, we see the emergence of AI-powered industry platforms that deliver:
-faster time to value (pushing industry standards, quicker go-live, etc)
-more targeted, operational solutions (tell me an industry that wants generic platforms!)
-Net: better outcomes & ROI value for customers
At the same time, this creates opportunities for companies across industries to evolve their own offerings in SaaS-like ways:
-recurring digital services & revenue
-data-driven insights
-New go-to-market and customer engagement models
This opens the door to innovative revenue models across SaaS & Industry:
-SaaS companies can develop smarter licensing, consumption, and platform pricing models - driving more predictable cloud revenue and deeper customer relationships. (I spend a bit of time thinking about this 😉)
-Industry organizations gain smarter, more tailored platforms that improve operations and decision-making - this presents the opportunity to dive deep into developing best-in-class GTM strategies and value-added services for their end customers.
Hence, from my perspective, SaaS isn’t going anywhere.
It’s evolving into something even more valuable: AI-enabled platforms that combine software, data, and industry expertise to drive better outcomes for customers and more durable growth for SaaS providers.
In a market that can sometimes feel murky, this evolution creates a real opportunity to leapfrog competition. The organizations that will stand out are those willing to invest thoughtfully in both technology and people, bringing together the best of SaaS innovation and industry expertise to clearly differentiate and lead in the years ahead. 🚀
So tell me, how are you seeing AI change SaaS and industry platforms in your world? Like my kids, I encourage you to tell me why I am wrong!
