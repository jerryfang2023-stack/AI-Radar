# Hang Ten Systems raises $32 million to help enterprises succeed with AI

## clean_text

Press Release · For Immediate Release
Hang Ten Systems raises $32 million to help enterprises succeed with AI
Palo Alto, Calif. · June 24, 2026
PALO ALTO, Calif., June 24, 2026 — Hang Ten Systems,
an enterprise AI services company, has raised $32 million in seed funding. The round
will support Hang Ten in building its team and expanding its work with global enterprises.
The company is led by Dr. Vishal Sikka, Co-founder and CEO, formerly CEO of Infosys and
Executive Board Member at SAP in charge of all products and technology. Hang Ten helps
large enterprises adopt AI to produce real results. Today, very few enterprises can use AI
to significant advantage; most are not, and indeed they take on cost and risk without the
benefit. Hang Ten exists to close this gap.
Hang Ten's focus is on software that runs the business. Generative AI is displacing the
traditional model of bending commercial off-the-shelf software (COTS) to enterprise needs
via configuration, customization, integration, and testing. AI code generation has
materially cut the marginal cost and timeframe for building advanced enterprise
capabilities. Hang Ten offers a different operating model — software built, changed, and run
at a fraction of the cost and time, on a continuous basis — with specialized capabilities in
enterprise transformations, finance, HR, and new product development, supported by an expert
FDE Bench, re-usable skills library, and agentic code generation.
The round was led by Mayfield, with a strategic investment from Aramco Ventures and
participation from a group of angel investors. Hang Ten is working with marquee customers
across several industries, including Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy and Fresenius on
AI-native project delivery.
"Every single enterprise will be transformed by AI. A few are already reaping massive
benefits, building in days what used to take years. But most are stuck at the starting line,
or worse, and the gap is widening every day. We are building Hang Ten to close that gap, and
help enterprises ride the AI wave. We bring together the rarest combination — frontier AI
expertise, decades of building the systems that enterprises actually run on, and the judgment
to deliver real value when everything is changing at once. I am excited to be working closely
with Mayfield, Aramco Ventures, and several renowned angel investors to help deliver on this
promise at scale."
— Dr. Vishal Sikka, CEO and Founder of Hang Ten
"At Mayfield, we invest in people first, and Vishal is one of the rare leaders who can make
enterprise AI actually work. We backed Vishal from inception because he has done this before
running products and technology at SAP and leading Infosys as CEO. Within a few years, the
gap between enterprises that use AI to real advantage and those that don't will define entire
industries. Hang Ten is focused on enterprise transformations to put companies on the right
side of that line."
— Navin Chaddha, Managing Partner at Mayfield
"Deployment of AI at enterprise scale is fast becoming the biggest challenge and opportunity
of our time. We are proud to join Vishal and the Hang Ten team in their seed round to go after
this generational opportunity. Their combination of Frontier AI experience, decades of
delivering enterprise solutions at scale, and deep enterprise relationships worldwide is rare,
and is what it will take to deliver on the promise of Enterprise AI."
— Mahdi Aladel, CEO of Aramco Ventures
"Every enterprise I know is looking for sound trusted guidance on AI and help with
dramatically improving major operational programs. I am very excited to see Vishal and his
team tackle this challenge with Hang Ten. We at SGRE are delighted to work with Hang Ten on
several strategic initiatives."
— Vinod Philip, CEO of Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy (SGRE)
Hang Ten is hiring across delivery, engineering, sales, and leadership. To learn more, visit
www.hangten.ai .

About Hang Ten Systems
Hang Ten Systems is an enterprise AI services company. It helps large enterprises adopt AI to
produce real results, using an AI-native model — agentic code generation, a reusable skills
library, and deep domain expertise — to build, change, and run enterprise software at lower
cost and on shorter timelines. Learn more at www.hangten.ai .
About Mayfield
Mayfield is an early-stage venture firm investing $3 billion in AI companies, with 70% of
investments at inception. For more than 56 years, we've partnered with founders from their
first idea to IPO and beyond, with 120 IPOs and 225+ acquisitions. We believe great companies
are built by people first and back founders building the future of AI. Learn more at
mayfield.com .

Aramco Ventures is the venture capital arm of Aramco, with $7.5 billion in committed capital
across four venturing programs spanning Strategic Venturing, Diversified Venturing through
Prosperity7, Domestic Venturing through Wa'ed Ventures, and Late-Stage Venturing.
Headquartered in Dhahran, with offices in North America, Europe, and Asia, the platform
invests across multiple stages in high-potential startups globally, while also supporting the
Kingdom's entrepreneurship and innovation ecosystem. Its investments focus on technologies
that advance industrial innovation, digital transformation, sustainability, and business
diversification.
Beyond capital, Aramco Ventures accelerates commercialization by connecting its portfolio
companies to deployment opportunities within Aramco and across global markets. For more
