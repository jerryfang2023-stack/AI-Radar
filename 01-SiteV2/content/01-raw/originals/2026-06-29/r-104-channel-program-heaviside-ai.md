# Channel Program — Heaviside AI

## clean_text

Heaviside is built for channel motion — not direct sales. Every SKU transacts on Azure Marketplace as a Private Offer, burns down the customer's existing Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment, and pays channel margin to the originating partner. Three named partners drive enterprise and federal deals today. ... Avanade is the Microsoft + Accenture joint venture and the enterprise transformation channel for Microsoft cloud. The Avanade Heaviside pipeline targets AmLaw 100 firms, F500 legal and compliance offices, financial services HQs, and life sciences enterprises that have stalled on commercial AI deployment. ... CDW Government is the federal reseller channel for civilian and defense agencies. The CDW + Heaviside joint pitch has been delivered against the GSAR 552.239-7001 mandate. CDW owns the federal task-order workflow, the IDIQ vehicles, and the contracting officer relationships. Heaviside ships the binary the procurement language describes. ... Every enterprise Azure customer carries an active Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment — a multi-year, multi-million-dollar spend obligation negotiated at the Enterprise Agreement level. MACC dollars are use-it-or-lose-it. Azure Marketplace Private Offers count against the MACC. Heaviside is sold through that channel. ... The reason regulated AI deals stall isn't technical — it's procurement. New AI spending in 2026 routes through new procurement cycles. New procurement cycles trigger six-month vendor risk reviews. Most deals die there. ... customer's CIO already has the budget ... Heaviside SKUs transact on Azure Marketplace as Private Offers — sometimes multi-party Private Offers that route channel margin through CDW or Avanade. The dollars come out of MACC. Microsoft burns down its commitment. The partner clips margin. Heaviside ships the receipt the Inspector General accepts. ... Every Heaviside SKU — from the $5,500 Pilot to the $250,000 Sovereign — is MACC-eligible. No procurement cycle. Zero-day deals. ... Avanade for enterprise transformation. CDW for federal task orders. Either way, the partner owns the customer relationship. ... in Azure Marketplace ... Two-click acceptance. MACC dollars debited automatically. No new procurement cycle. No new vendor onboarding. ... Multi-party Private Offers carry transparent margin to the partner. The $250K Sovereign tier is the same margin structure as the $5.5K Pilot. The partner gets paid on the deal they originated, period. ... The procurement cycle is the deal-killer in regulated AI. MACC eliminates it.
