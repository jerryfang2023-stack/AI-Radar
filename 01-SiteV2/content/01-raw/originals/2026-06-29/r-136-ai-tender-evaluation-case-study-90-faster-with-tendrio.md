Eldis Accelerates Tender Evaluation by 90% With Tendrio
Eldis is a technology company operating in highly regulated sectors, where every tender requires detailed technical and contractual compliance. Their team previously relied on fully manual review of extensive RFP documentation, which limited capacity and slowed down decision-making.
Download Full Case Study (PDF)
Book a demo
The Challenge
Before adopting Tendrio, Eldis evaluated tenders manually. The process was time-consuming, required multiple experts, and made it difficult to scale. Reviewing a single tender often took several days, and the company could manage only 5–10 tenders per month. With increasing documentation volume, maintaining accuracy and efficiency became challenging.
The Solution
Tendrio introduced AI-driven document matching that automated the most repetitive parts of analysis. The team quickly adopted Tendrio across sales, engineering, and compliance. A short onboarding and pilot phase enabled full deployment within weeks.
Key Results
90% faster evaluation
3× more tenders processed with the same team
Up to 35% less manual review work
20–30% HR cost savings
Faster Go/No-Go decisions: 30–60 minutes instead of days
Higher accuracy and fewer overlooked specifications
Quote from Eldis
“Tendrio allowed us to evaluate tenders dramatically faster. Tasks that used to take days now take under an hour — with more consistent outputs.”
Erik Šmol
Bid Manager, Eldis
Conclusion
The implementation of Tendrio enabled Eldis to streamline tender evaluation, increase capacity, and reduce internal workload while maintaining high accuracy.
