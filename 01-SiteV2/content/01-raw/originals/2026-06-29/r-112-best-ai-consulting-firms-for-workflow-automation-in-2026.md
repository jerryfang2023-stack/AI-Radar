What Is AI Consulting for Workflow Automation?
AI consulting for workflow automation helps businesses assess, design, build, and deploy AI-powered systems that replace manual, repetitive processes with intelligent, adaptive automation. The distinction from basic Robotic Process Automation (RPA) matters here. RPA executes fixed, rule-based tasks. AI adds decision-making through machine learning, enabling systems to analyze unstructured data, recognize patterns, and handle dynamic scenarios without human intervention.
Common workflows targeted for automation include:
Invoice processing and accounts payable
Document routing and classification
Compliance monitoring and audit trails
Customer onboarding and identity verification
Data entry and validation
Reporting and analytics pipelines
