AWS GenAIIC Partner Agent Factory: New AI agents now in AWS Marketplace
According to Gartner , the average Global Fortune 500 enterprise will have over 150,000 AI agents in use by 2028, yet moving AI projects from prototype to production at scale remains a key challenge. As organizations shift from agentic AI experimentation to production deployment, they need practical ways to reduce development complexity, bridge talent gaps, and accelerate time-to-value for their AI investments.
The AWS Generative AI Innovation Center (GenAIIC) Partner Agent Factory (PAF) helps address these challenges. PAF brings together the technical expertise of the Generative AI Innovation Center and AWS Partner industry specialization to deliver validated AI agent solutions. You can now explore and deploy the newest AI agents developed through PAF co-innovation in AWS Marketplace .
“The Partner Agent Factory turns co-innovation into customer value by pairing the technical depth of GenAIIC with our partners’ domain expertise to build AI agents that solve real challenges, from automating clinical workflows and governing AI at scale to transforming video archives into actionable insights. Now live in AWS Marketplace, these solutions give organizations a proven path from agentic AI ambition to measurable business outcomes.”
– Taimur Rashid, Director and Head of Generative Innovation and Delivery, AWS.
In this post, we introduce the latest PAF solutions and explain how you can discover these solutions and co-innovate through the program.
From co-innovation to customer value
Organizations that accelerate the path from proof of concept to production gain a competitive advantage. Partner Agent Factory is a structured co-innovation program that brings together the Generative AI Innovation Center and AWS Partners to solve real business problems, compressing the deployment timeline with validated AI agent solutions powered by Amazon Bedrock , Strands Agents , and Amazon Bedrock AgentCore . Here’s what sets PAF solutions apart:
Proven methodology – PAF validates each agentic solution through a structured Generative AI Innovation Center co-innovation framework derived from thousands of engagements with enterprise customers.
Designed for industry-specific challenges – AWS Partners bring extensive domain expertise to address challenges across industries.
Powered by the latest AWS AI services – You benefit from Amazon Web Services (AWS) agentic AI solutions built with Amazon Bedrock, Strands Agents, and Amazon Bedrock AgentCore.
Streamlined procurement – You get simplified procurement, consolidated billing, and purchases through AWS Marketplace that count toward your existing AWS spending commitments.
Introducing the latest PAF solutions
The latest Partner Agent Factory cohorts span seven agentic AI solutions from four AWS Partners, covering multilingual document analysis, video intelligence, AI governance, customer service, financial services, retail optimization, and clinical documentation. You can now access these solutions in AWS Marketplace.
MegazoneCloud: AIR Document Translation
AIR Document Translation (ADT) is an AI-powered multi-agent solution that streamlines enterprise document translation from end to end. From document analysis and guideline generation to translation, quality verification, and professionally formatted output, ADT delivers consistent, high-quality results with minimal manual effort while maintaining expert oversight at every stage. AIR Document Translation in AWS Marketplace .
MegazoneCloud: SPACE LENS
SPACE LENS is a video intelligence solution that transforms untapped video archives into high-value business assets. Using natural language queries, SPACE LENS automatically orchestrates the optimal analysis path to extract actionable insights and structured data from video content, eliminating the complexity of model selection while significantly reducing time and costs. SPACE LENS in AWS Marketplace .
MegazoneCloud: AIR Agent Governance
AIR Agent Governance (AAG) is an enterprise AI agent governance platform built on Amazon Bedrock AgentCore and Strands Agents. AAG enables organizations to centrally manage the full AI agent lifecycle, from registration and approval through deployment, operations, audit, and cost governance, helping enterprises scale AI agents with confidence and control. AAG in AWS Marketplace .
“Co-innovating with the AWS Generative AI Innovation Center (GenAIIC) through the Partner Agent Factory (PAF) program was a transformative experience for MegazoneCloud. We collaborated across architecture, governance, and go-to-market strategy, rapidly evolving agentic AI patterns into enterprise-ready solutions. Combining MegazoneCloud’s generative AI capabilities and industry experience with the deep expertise of GenAIIC enables us to deliver solutions that address real business challenges for enterprise customers.” – Doug Yeum, President and CEO, MegazoneCloud
Nubiral: Nubi Agentic Concierge
Nubi Agentic Concierge is an AI-powered virtual agent that autonomously resolves repetitive customer inquiries across web, WhatsApp, Slack, and other channels. The solution handles thousands of simultaneous conversations in real time, with responses grounded in your organization’s documents and APIs, and an auditable reasoning chain for regulated industries. Nubi Agentic Concierge in AWS Marketplace .
“Collaborating with the AWS Generative AI Innovation Center (GenAIIC) through the Partner Agent Factory (PAF) was a genuinely eye-opening experience—a true example of co-innovation turning into real business impact. Together, we built the Nubi Agentic Concierge by combining Nubiral’s enterprise expertise with the expert guidance of GenAIIC. We reached 98.9% retrieval precision and resolved 70% of interactions autonomously, demonstrating what enterprise AI can deliver in the region. Latin America’s enterprises are ready for AI tailored to their needs, and this collaboration with AWS empowered us to bring that to market.” – Maximiliano Giacri, Co-founder and CEO, Nubiral
Loka: MedEncAI – Encounter Assistant
Loka MedEncAI – Encounter Assistant is an autonomous clinical documentation agent built on Amazon Bedrock AgentCore and Strands Agents. MedEncAI transforms physician–patient encounter audio into structured, coded, evidence-linked clinical notes in under 2 minutes, with built-in hallucination verification and self-correction against a clinical rubric. MedEncAI – Encounter Assistant in AWS Marketplace .
“The Partner Agent Factory is exactly the kind of program the industry needs to hold clinical documentation to a higher standard. The GenAIIC team brought rigorous, multistage reviews across architecture, evaluation methods, and reliability, with feedback that was detailed, attentive, and genuinely elevated the outcome. For Loka, that collaboration helped shape MedEncAI, our agentic medical documentation solution now available in AWS Marketplace. PAF gave us confidence that what we’re shipping to ISVs has been rigorously reviewed by a team that holds the bar as high as we do.” – Paul Englert, Chief Technology Officer, Loka
NorthBay: Agentic Financial Intelligence Engine (AFIE)
The Agentic Financial Intelligence Engine (AFIE) transforms natural language questions into actionable insights across heterogeneous financial data sources. AFIE delivers a comprehensive enterprise view that surfaces opportunities, risks, and operational improvements. C-suite executives and key stakeholders gain personalized, interactive insights with transparent, verifiable logic behind every recommendation. AFIE in AWS Marketplace .
NorthBay: Retail AI Agentic Platform
The Retail AI Agentic Platform synthesizes diverse data sources into a unified enterprise view for mid-market and enterprise retailers. It surfaces stockout risks, promotional opportunities, and pricing optimizations across the retail value chain. Merchandising, supply chain, and operations teams gain actionable recommendations to move faster on demand, inventory, and pricing decisions. Retail AI Agentic Platform in AWS Marketplace .
“With the technical expertise and structured guidance of GenAIIC at every milestone, PAF enabled us to advance two agentic AI solutions from concept to marketplace readiness through deep collaboration. AFIE provides financial services leaders with an interactive view across operational data, and our retail platform enables consumer goods teams to act decisively across demand, inventory, and pricing. PAF provides us the structure and rigor to deliver with confidence.” – John Flavin, Chief Operating Officer, NorthBay Solutions
