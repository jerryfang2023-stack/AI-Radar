We began with the logo.
We started with sketches, then refined the idea through structure. Mathematics became our guide. Balance informed every decision.

That process gave us the centerpiece of our new identity: a logo rooted in Bauhaus philosophy, where form follows function and every element serves a purpose.
The world’s best AI models become more valuable when they work together. We applied that same idea across our entire identity.
Our new typeface echoes the geometric discipline of the logo while remaining approachable, balanced, and versatile.
Our expanded color palette was designed with equal intention. Bright colors create moments of energy and optimism. Deeper tones bring confidence, focus, and balance.
Even light mode and dark mode were approached as distinct environments, intentionally integrated across web, mobile, and every product experience.
Together, these decisions mark the beginning of OpenRouter’s next chapter.
An expression of the principles that define us.
A declaration of the opportunities we intend to create.
For developers.
For enterprises.
