# Monogram Raises $40M Seed

## clean_text

Monogram Raises $40M Seed
Monogram, an AI startup focused on visual interfaces, has announced a $40M seed funding round. The company is introducing a new general-purpose AI application designed to move beyond traditional text-based chat interfaces by generating interactive visual UIs in real-time.
Investors
The funding round was led by DST Global Partners and Lux Capital. Additional participants in the round included Conviction, SOMA Capital, Gradient Ventures, e2vc, Maxitech, Arthur Mensch, Logan Green, Karim Atiyeh, Garry Tan, Pete Flint, Mario Schlosser, Othman Laraki, Lenny Rachitsky, and Charlie Songhurst.
Monogram Use of Funds
Monogram plans to use the new capital to scale the development and distribution of its visual AI application designed to make interactions with artificial intelligence more intuitive and interactive for everyday use cases.
About Monogram
Founded in 2026 by Eren Bali with Edouard Tabet and Murat Akbal, Monogram is an artificial intelligence startup focused on reimagining how humans interact with AI by replacing traditional text-based chat interfaces with dynamic, visual experiences.
