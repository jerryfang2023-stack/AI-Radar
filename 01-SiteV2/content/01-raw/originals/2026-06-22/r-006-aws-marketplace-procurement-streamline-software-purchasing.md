Procurement features in AWS Marketplace
Custom terms and pricing
Private Offers and Channel Partner Private Offers enable you to receive custom software pricing and terms from a third-party that is not publicly available in AWS Marketplace. Meet your software procurement needs with custom terms, volume pricing, and flexible payment options negotiated privately with your preferred independent software vendor (ISV) or Channel Partner.

Accelerate transaction speed with pre-negotiated contract templates that are designed to address the fundamental needs of buyers.
