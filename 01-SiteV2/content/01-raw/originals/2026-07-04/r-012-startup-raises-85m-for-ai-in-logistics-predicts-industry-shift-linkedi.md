$110M raised in 5 months.
A one-year-old startup just raised $85M in a Series A round led by Redpoint Ventures, with participation from 8VC, Shopify Ventures, Autotech Ventures, and others.
That tells us investors believe:
– Workflow-native AI (trained on logistics data + processes) creates defensible moats.
– Adoption curves are faster when AI feels like a teammate, not another SaaS login.
– The TAM is huge. Logistics is a trillion-dollar industry with margin pressure and labor bottlenecks.
Logistics is just one example.
Similar vertical plays are emerging in:
Legal → Contract review agents.
Finance → AI underwriters, wealth management agents.
Construction → AI safety monitors and project schedulers.
Healthcare → Ambient clinical documentation, revenue cycle AI.
Augment’s raise is a datapoint in the larger trend:
📌 Capital is moving from broad AI platforms into applied, industry-specific AI.

The Jolt team—Spektor, Jon Reynolds , and Carlos Kelly —will join Ramp’s engineering platform unit, focusing on applied AI, infrastructure, and customer-facing financial workflows.
