Now? They’re asking for companies that make humans optional in entire workflows.
Here’s what jumped out at me:
Cursor for Product Managers → AI that doesn’t just ship features, but decides what to ship. The loop is closing.

AI Guidance for Physical Work → Real-time coaching for plumbers, nurses, factory workers. The Matrix “I know kung fu” moment, but for blue-collar jobs.
Stablecoin Financial Services → The missing middle layer between TradFi and DeFi, now that regulation is finally catching up.
The meta-theme is unmistakable:
AI-native companies that can be built 10x faster and cheaper than ever before, attacking problems that used to require massive teams and decades of legacy systems.
This isn’t hype. It’s YC saying: the era of bolting AI onto old businesses is over. The winners will be the ones that rebuild the business from the ground up with AI.
If you’re building in any of these areas (or even adjacent), this is the strongest bat signal I’ve seen in years.
