## 原始摘要 / 采集文本

Microsoft Azure Marketplace # # WeManage eProcurement # WeManage eProcurement ## Procure-to-pay solution with best practice WeManage eProcurement is designed to help financial system to extend procure-to-pay automation to unlimited employees and vendors. WeManage eProcurement is ideal for customers who are already using Microsoft Dynamics ERP, such as Business Central, Microsoft Office 365 applications like SharePoint, Teams. Your procurement can choose to deploy selected or all functions: vendor registration, vendor management, vendor performance evaluation, purchase requisition, e-quotation, tender management, e-tender, e-bidding, award with PO and contract management, e-award, e-fulfilment and e-invoice. Procurement department can greatly benefited from the fully web eProcurement solution. The self-service functionality relief a lot of the data entries effort. Every stakeholder can participate in the tender evaluation, vendor performance evaluation, reviewing and approving procurement request from anywhere, anytime. The unique strength of WeManage eProcurement is in the automation of smart evaluation computation, plus the 360 degree view of the vendor profile and activities. There are 2 editions of WeManage eProcurement: 1) Software as a Service (SaaS Edition) 2) Customized Project (Tailored Edition) Each edition provides 3 plans, differentiate by the number of legal entity / company. SaaS edition has additional free plan. a) Free Plan (only for SaaS) b) Standard Plan (1 company) c) Professional Plan (3 companies) d) Enterprise Plan (10 companies) eProcurement BrochurePlans & PricingHomepage / score=52.667 / query=AI Agent funding enterprise customers / intent=find_startups / path=procurement_marketplace
