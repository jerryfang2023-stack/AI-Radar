AI Digital Workers for Supply Chain
Lumari is the world's first AI-powered procurement automation platform purpose-built for direct materials and supply chain execution.
Backed by Y Combinator, Lumari is a well-funded startup that deploys 100s of always-on AI agents that run sourcing workflows, manage RFQs, expedite purchase orders, and coordinate supplier communication end-to-end -- compressing cycle times and giving teams full visibility into active orders, RFQs, and supplier performance.
Founded by engineers from Google, Tesla, Amazon, and Stripe who have built and scaled supply chain systems serving millions of users.
Built to operate directly on top of existing tools and ERP systems, Lumari helps procurement teams move faster, eliminate operational bottlenecks, and build more resilient supply chains. No rip-and-replace, no supplier onboarding. Lumari handles the manual chase: quote follow-ups, PO confirmations, delivery status updates, and risk escalation.
Lumari helps companies from Fortune 500s to fast-growing startups automate work from sourcing to delivery, streamline supplier communication and order management, and bring full visibility to their procurement operations.
To learn more check out https://lumari.ai/

Hey everyone! We’re Sam and Eshani , the co-founders of Lumari 🔥
TL;DR: Lumari is AI-powered procurement automation for direct materials and supply chain. We deploy 100s of always-on AI agents that run sourcing workflows, manage RFQs, expedite purchase orders, and coordinate supplier communication end-to-end.
The Problem ❌
Supply chain teams are stuck in reactive mode.
Buyers spend their days copy-pasting between email, spreadsheets, and ERPs, chasing supplier quotes, following up on late POs, and manually entering delivery updates
Missed delivery dates and poor visibility lead to expediting costs, production delays, and revenue risk
Existing tools require suppliers to adopt new portals and force rigid workflows that don't reflect how teams actually operate
There's too much operational work and not enough time for strategic decision-making
Lumari’s Solution ✨
Lumari brings autonomous agents to supply chain and acts as a force multiplier for the team.
Automates the manual chase across the entire procurement lifecycle, from RFQs to PO tracking to supplier follow-ups
Compresses cycle times and gives teams full visibility into active orders, RFQs, and supplier performance
Operates directly on top of existing tools (ERP, email, Slack, Excel) with no rip-and-replace and no supplier onboarding
Teams are always in control with configurable guardrails and approval layers for every action
Customize your agent behavior and configuration in plain English
We partner closely with every customer to adapt our agents to their specific processes and systems, from Fortune 500s to fast-growing scale-ups.
The Team
Sam Lamba is an ex-Google engineering lead who led the development of AI-driven products that scaled to millions of weekly enterprise users, and has received 2 AI patents. Sam also previously worked at Amazon and Tesla and holds a degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Computer Science from Duke University.
