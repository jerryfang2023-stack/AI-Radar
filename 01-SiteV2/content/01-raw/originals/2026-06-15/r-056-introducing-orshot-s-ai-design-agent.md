Blog Updates Introducing Orshot's AI Design Agent
Tell the AI what you want to create, and it will generate customizable templates and designs in seconds. Build social posts, ads, and certificates with simple text prompts
Rishi Mohan Mar 01, 2026 2 min read
Copy Link Ask AI
Orshot has always been about making bulk image and PDF generation efficient and fast. But creating the perfect baseline template from scratch can still take time. Enter the Orshot Design Agent .
Our new AI-powered agent lives right inside your Orshot Studio and builds templates for you. Just describe what you need, and it handles the layout, typography, colors, and element positioning automatically.
What is the Orshot Design Agent?
The Design Agent is an interactive AI assistant integrated directly into the canvas. Instead of manually dragging and dropping shapes, text, and images, you can simply chat with the AI. Ask it to scaffold a layout, insert dynamic placeholders for the API, or re-theme an entire design instantly.
Once the agent generates your template, you can still manually tweak any detail or immediately connect the template to our REST API, Zapier, or Make.
How to Use It
Open a new or existing template in the Orshot Studio .
Click on the Design Agent / Chat panel.
Type your request into the chat box. (e.g., "Create a modern real estate Instagram post..." )
Watch the agent drop elements and build the template right on your canvas.
Ask for revisions (e.g., "Make the background darker" ) or manually fine-tune the finalized canvas.
Actionable Workflows You Can Try Today
The Design Agent isn't just a party trick—it's built to accelerate your content automation pipelines. Instead of starting from a blank canvas, let the AI scaffold your layout, then immediately connect it to your favorite automation tools for bulk generation. Here is how you can use it:
Automate E-commerce Product Banners: Ask the agent to "Create a minimal product sale banner with a large image placeholder and a price tag." Once generated, you can use our REST API to automate e-commerce product images matching your catalog.
Bulk Generate Certificates: Prompt the agent with "Design a modern course completion certificate with placeholders for student name and date." From there, scale it up and bulk generate PDF certificates via API or Make.
No-Code Social Media Pipelines: Generate a base template for Twitter or Instagram quotes. Then, connect your new template via our Make integration or Zapier to automatically generate and post new images every time you add a row in Google Sheets or Airtable.
Programmatic SEO Graphics: Need thousands of unique OG images for your content? Ask the agent to "Create an Open Graph image with a dynamic title and background." Once complete, use it as part of your programmatic SEO workflow rather than doing it by hand.
Scale Any Visual Content: Combine the Design Agent with automated content creation tools to go from idea to hundreds of branded variations without manual design work.
The Orshot Design Agent is available now on all plans. Head over to your workspace, spin up a new template, and let the AI do the heavy lifting!
