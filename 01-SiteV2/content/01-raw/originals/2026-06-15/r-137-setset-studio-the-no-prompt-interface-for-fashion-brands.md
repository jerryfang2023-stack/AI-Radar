Setset Studio: the no-prompt interface for fashion brands
Product Launch | May 2026
Introducing Setset Studio
Today we’re announcing the release of Setset Studio: the no-prompt interface for fashion brands to create studio-quality AI visuals at scale. A self-serve platform built so any brand can produce studio-grade fashion imagery at scale. Start for free now at setset.ai.
The best fashion brands we’ve worked with share one thing in common: their creative ambition outpaces what traditional production can deliver.
Over the past two years, Setset has worked directly with brands to produce on-model imagery, wholesale catalogs, and campaign visuals using AI. No traditional shoot required. We grew entirely through word of mouth, partnering with teams at forward-thinking brands like TWP, MZ Wallace and Tombolo, where timelines are tight and the margin for error is small. Together we’ve shipped thousands of product and wholesale pages, cut production costs, and improved both quality and product page engagement.
The bottleneck isn’t creativity. It’s production.
Content timelines don’t match launch calendars. Final images differ from the brief. Budgets get spent, and teams are left with assets they can’t use.
Along the way we had to turn away brands who didn’t have the budget for a bespoke solution. So we built a self-serve platform to solve this.
How Studio works
Upload your design files or product samples. Setset generates on-model imagery, lifestyle shots, PDP assets, and video in minutes. No prompting required. Always organized. Output is consistent, on-brand, and ready to publish.
Built for teams that:
• Run lean and need to move fast
• Manage significant SKU volume across seasons
• Already use AI and expect production-level output
Free-credit pilot
We’re opening a free-credit pilot for a small group of brands. If your team is shipping at scale and you want to see what Setset can do, we’d love to talk.
Start for free
