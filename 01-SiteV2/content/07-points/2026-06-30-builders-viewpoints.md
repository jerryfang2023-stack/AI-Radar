If a closed stack is always perpetually at the frontier by a wide margin, then being vertically integrated, and gate keeping in the US can work. Because you always have control over who gets access to the best technology, and it will be in high enough demand that it always favors you. If, however, open weights AI can remain a close second to frontier intelligence, then the equation reverses. Depending on your belief on how close to the frontier open weights can remain -and similarly what percentage of tokens will go through the frontier vs. everything else- your opinion will be different on how to regulate and control AI.
