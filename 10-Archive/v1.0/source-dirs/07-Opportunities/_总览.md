## 需复盘提醒

> `next_review` 已到期或近 7 天到期的机会。

```dataview
TABLE
  priority AS "优先级",
  status AS "阶段",
  next_review AS "复盘日",
  date(today) - next_review AS "逾期天数"
FROM "01-WaveSight/07-Opportunities"
WHERE 
  contains(tags, "AI创业机会") AND 
  file.name != "_总览" AND
  next_review <= date(today) + dur(7 days)
SORT next_review ASC
```
