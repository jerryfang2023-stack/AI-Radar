# AI Brief Quality Gate

Status: isolation gate.

## Required Checks

- Issue has `id`, `title`, `period`, `issueType`, and `executiveSummary`.
- Weekly issue contains top triples, rising/cooling/controversial heat points, evidence summary, counter-evidence summary, and evidence gaps.
- Every top heat point traces to HeatEvidence.
- Each core judgment states boundary and uncertainty.
- No investment, operation, cooperation, or procurement instruction is given.
- Public summary and member content are clearly separated in future product work.

## Blocking Failures

- Issue is only a ranking list.
- Evidence summary cannot trace to source assets.
- Missing counter-evidence.
- Certainty or command language.
