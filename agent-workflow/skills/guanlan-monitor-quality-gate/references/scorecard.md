# Guanlan Monitor Quality Gate Scorecard

The score is an operational diagnostic. It never overrides executable hard gates and it is not a publication condition.

## Dimensions

1. Source integrity: original-source evidence, hashes/excerpts, failed-source and fallback visibility.
2. Content quality: readable full text, extraction quality and contamination checks.
3. Coverage scope: Raw/Pool/Core targets, source breadth and concentration diagnostics.
4. Keyword compliance: search-path attempts and unresolved coverage gaps.
5. Strategic alignment: concrete AI commercial actions and importance mix.
6. Evidence readiness: minimum routed/Core source-backed supply to attempt Card generation.

## Outcome

- `passed`: executable evidence-supply hard gates pass.
- `failed`: one or more executable evidence-supply hard gates fail.

Raw 150, Pool 75, routed Pool 60, Core 30, keyword breadth, importance mix, provider status and score remain diagnostics. Optional semantic QC can identify additional repair work, but no QC report or freshness check is required for release.

After this gate, Card generation, dedupe, editorial quality and unified frontstage gates make their own stage-owned decisions.
