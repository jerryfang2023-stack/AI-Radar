# Good Community Lead Example

Community Intelligence can preserve leads and tactics without promoting them into verified business facts.

```yaml
---
date: 2026-06-12
lead_id: CI-20260612-01
source_platform: logged-in-community
source_url: https://community.example.com/post/abc
source_actor_type: operator
category: workflow_tactic
archive_path: 01-SiteV2/knowledge/04-Community-Intelligence/2026-06-12/CI-20260612-01.md
verification_status: community_lead_only
business_signal_promotion: blocked_until_raw_pool_verified
---

lead_summary: "A practitioner reports a concrete tactic, blocker, or opportunity seen in a community discussion."
evidence_boundary: "This is a community-sourced lead. It is not yet a verified product, funding, or case fact."
next_check: "Look for official source, customer case page, pricing page, procurement note, or credible media confirmation."
```

Pass criteria:

- The source and archive path are preserved.
- The item stays inside the Community Intelligence lane.
- The evidence boundary blocks direct promotion to Cards, graph edges, or trend candidates.
