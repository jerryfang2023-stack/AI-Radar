# Community Intelligence Lane Contract

The Community Intelligence lane owns:

- local logged-in community collection;
- community frontstage data;
- daily snapshots and Obsidian archive outputs;
- community data gate reports;
- community automation PR publication.

It must not own:

- Business Signal Cards;
- relationship graph or trend-candidate evidence;
- First-Line Viewpoints data;
- verified business facts unless separately promoted through Raw / Pool.
