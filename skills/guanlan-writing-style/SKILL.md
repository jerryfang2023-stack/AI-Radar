# 观澜AI文风

Use this skill whenever copy must sound like 观澜AI: cold-headed, judgment-led, human, and close to real business work. The copy should feel written by someone who understands AI, commercial pressure, and how companies actually change.

## Core Voice

Write in a **冷静、有判断、有温度的商业内参风**.

The voice should carry four instincts:

- A business journalist's sensitivity to change.
- An industry researcher's restraint.
- A consultant's sense of tradeoffs.
- A founder/operator's feel for messy reality.

Do not sound like an AI news site, tool directory, marketing page, consulting whitepaper, or model-generated summary.

## First Principles

Keep the writing close to the reader's real questions:

- What does this have to do with my business?
- Which industry, role, workflow, cost, risk, or team habit might change?
- Where could a company start without buying noise?
- What is still too early, too vague, or too risky?

Prefer short, specific, human sentences. It is better to be incomplete but true than complete and hollow.

## Anti-AI Taste Rules

Avoid obvious AI-generated texture:

- Do not mechanically structure with "首先 / 其次 / 最后 / 总之 / 综上所述".
- Do not repeat the same sentence frame across a page, especially "不是……而是……", "这意味着……", "核心在于……", "对企业来说……".
- Do not stack abstract claims such as "AI 正在重塑各行各业", "企业必须拥抱 AI", "未来已来".
- Do not pad paragraphs with concept words: 赋能、闭环、生态、范式、底座、抓手、价值链路、数智化升级.
- Do not over-complete every answer. Let some sentences breathe.

When rewriting AI-ish copy, keep the fact, remove the performance.

## Judgment Boundaries

Have a point of view, but do not boss the reader around.

Use:

- "值得继续看"
- "更适合先小范围试"
- "证据还不够"
- "风险在交付和数据"
- "这件事可能先影响销售、客服、内容、运营这类高频流程"

Avoid:

- "必须马上行动"
- "确定性机会"
- "不学就会被淘汰"
- "抢占红利"
- "弯道超车"
- "一键提升效率"

A good 观澜 sentence gives the reader a clearer next thought, not a louder emotion.

## Rewrite Method

When copy feels like AI, marketing, or a news digest:

1. Preserve the fact. Do not invent evidence.
2. Cut slogans, hype, and generic correctness.
3. Find the commercial meaning behind the fact.
4. Move toward industry, role, workflow, cost, efficiency, risk, or adoption.
5. Add one real judgment or caution.
6. Vary rhythm. Do not let every paragraph use the same skeleton.
7. Remove any sentence that has no new information.

## Column Voice

### 今日要点

Sound like a morning note for a decision-maker. Short, steady, and useful.

Answer what changed today, why it matters, and which industry, role, or workflow may feel it first.

Good texture:

> 今天更值得关注的，不是某个模型参数又提升了多少。真正的变化是，AI 正在从单点工具，慢慢进入企业流程。

### 关键信号

Do not stop at "what happened." Explain why this belongs in 观澜AI.

Good texture:

> 这条信息表面上是一款新产品发布。放到企业里看，它更像一个岗位动作被系统接管的早期信号。

### 机会解码

Do not call everything an opportunity. Say who it fits, who it does not fit, what must already be true, and where the risk sits.

Good texture:

> 这个方向值得看，但不适合所有企业马上跟进。它更适合流程相对标准、数据愿意沉淀、结果可以衡量的团队。

### 商业内参

Write like a briefing for people who make decisions. Avoid public-account emotion and research-report jargon.

Cover: core judgment, key facts, commercial impact, industry / role / workflow mapping, migration possibility, opportunity, risk, and 观澜 conclusion.

### 商业热力图

Use data language, but keep judgment visible. Explain why certain scenarios are hotter.

Good texture:

> 本期热力主要集中在销售线索处理、客服知识库和内容生产流程。它们的共同点是任务高频、标准化程度高、结果容易衡量。

## Microcopy

Use calm actions:

- 查看今日判断
- 阅读商业信号
- 查看影响路径
- 进入机会分析
- 阅读完整内参
- 查看热力图
- 生成企业诊断

Avoid sales pressure:

- 立即抢占红利
- 马上逆袭
- 一键颠覆
- 不看就晚了
- 开启财富密码
- 立即弯道超车

## Before / After Pattern

Bad:

> 企业应通过 AI Agent 构建业务流程自动化闭环，实现组织能力重构。

Better:

> AI 真正进入企业，不是多一个工具图标，而是原来靠人盯、靠人催、靠人整理的流程，开始有一部分可以被系统接过去。

Bad:

> 还在观望 AI 的老板，已经输在起跑线。

Better:

> 真正拉开差距的，可能不是谁先买了工具，而是谁先把 AI 放进真实业务里试了一轮。

## Final Check

Before finishing, scan the copy and ask:

- Does it sound like a real person, not a model answer?
- Is there a concrete industry, role, workflow, cost, adoption, or risk angle?
- Is the judgment clear without being arrogant?
- Did we remove filler and repeated sentence frames?
- Would a business owner feel less noisy after reading it?
